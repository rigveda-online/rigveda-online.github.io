HYMN LIII. PŪṢAN.

वयमुvayamu तवाtvā पथसpathas पतेpate रथंrathaṃ नna वाजसातयेvājasātaye |

धियेdhiye पूषन्नयुज्महिpūṣannayujmahi ||
LORD of the path, O Pūṣan, we have yoked and bound thee to our hymn,
Even as a car, to win the prize.
6.53.1
अभिabhi नोno नर्यंnaryaṃ वसुvasu वीरंvīraṃ परयतदक्षिणमprayatadakṣiṇam |

वामंvāmaṃ गर्हपतिंghṛhapatiṃ नयnaya ||
Bring us the wealth that men require, a manly master of a house,
Free-handed with the liberal meed.
6.53.2
अदित्सन्तंaditsantaṃ चिदाघ्र्णेcidāghṛṇe पूषनpūṣan दानायdānāya चोदयcodaya |

पणेश्चिदpaṇeścid विम्रदाvimradā मनःmanaḥ ||
Even him who would not give, do thou,
O glowing Pūṣan, urge to give,
And make the niggard's soul grow soft.
6.53.3
विvi पथोpatho वाजसातयेvājasātaye चिनुहिcinuhi विvi मर्धोmṛdho जहिjahi |

साधन्तामुग्रsādhantāmughra नोno धियःdhiyaḥ ||
Clear paths that we may win the prize; scatter our enemies afar.
Strong God, be all our thoughts fulfilled.
6.53.4
परिpari तर्न्धिtṛndhi पणीनामारयाpaṇīnāmārayā हर्दयाhṛdayā कवेkave |

अथेमस्मभ्यंathemasmabhyaṃ रन्धयrandhaya ||
Penetrate with an awl, O Sage, the hearts of avaricious churls,
And make them subject to our will.
6.53.5
विvi पूषन्नारयाpūṣannārayā तुदtuda पणेरिछpaṇericha हर्दिhṛdi परियमpriyam |

अथेंatheṃ ...... ||
Thrust with thine awl, O Pūṣan: seek that which the niggard's heart holds dear,
And make him subject to our will.
6.53.6
आā रिखrikha किकिराkikirā कर्णुkṛṇu पणीनांpaṇīnāṃ हर्दयाhṛdayā कवेkave |

अथेंatheṃ ...... ||
Tear up and read in pieces, Sage, the hearts of avaricious churls,
And make them subject to our will.
6.53.7
यांyāṃ पूषनpūṣan बरह्मचोदनीमारांbrahmacodanīmārāṃ बिभर्ष्याघ्र्णेbibharṣyāghṛṇe |

तयाtayā समस्यsamasya हर्दयमाhṛdayamā रिखrikha किकिराkikirā कर्णुkṛṇu ||
Thou, glowing Pūṣan, carriest an awl that urges men to prayer;
Therewith do thou tear up and rend to shreds the heart of every one.
6.53.8
याyā तेte अष्ट्राaṣṭrā गोोपशाघ्र्णेghoopaśāghṛṇe पशुसाधनीpaśusādhanī |

तस्यास्तेtasyāste सुम्नमीमहेsumnamīmahe ||
Thou bearest, glowing Lord! a goad with horny point that guides the cows
Thence do we seek thy gift of bliss.
6.53.9
उतuta नोno गोषणिंghoṣaṇiṃ धियमश्वसांdhiyamaśvasāṃ वाजसामुतvājasāmuta |

नर्वतnṛvat कर्णुहिkṛṇuhi वीतयेvītaye ||
And make this hymn of ours produce kine, horses, and a store of wealth
For our delight and use as men.
6.53.10
