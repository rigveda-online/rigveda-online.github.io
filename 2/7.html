HYMN VII. AGNI.

शरेष्ठंśreṣṭhaṃ यविष्ठyaviṣṭha भारताग्नेbhāratāghne दयुमन्तमाdyumantamā भरbhara |

वसोvaso पुरुस्प्र्हंpuruspṛhaṃ रयिमrayim ||
VASU, thou most youthful God, Bhārata, Agni, bring us wealth,
Excellent, splendid, much-desired.
2.7.1
माmā नोno अरातिरीशतarātirīśata देवस्यdevasya मर्त्यस्यmartyasya चca |

पर्षिparṣi तस्याtasyā उतद्विषःutadviṣaḥ ||
Let no malignity prevail against us, either God's or man's.
Save us from this and enmity.
2.7.2
विश्वाviśvā उतuta तवयाtvayā वयंvayaṃ धाराdhārā उदन्याudanyā इवiva |

अतिati गाहेमहिghāhemahi दविषःdviṣaḥ ||
So through thy favour may we force through all our enemies a way,
As ’twere through streaming water-floods.
2.7.3
शुचिःśuciḥ पावकpāvaka वन्द्यो.अग्नेvandyo.aghne बर्हदbṛhad विvi रोचसेrocase |

तवंtvaṃ घर्तेभिराहुतःghṛtebhirāhutaḥ ||
Thou, Purifier Agni, high shinest forth, bright, adorable,
When worshipped with the sacred oil.
2.7.4
तवंtvaṃ नोno असिasi भारताग्नेbhāratāghne वशाभिरुक्षभिःvaśābhirukṣabhiḥ |

अष्टापदीभिराहुतःaṣṭāpadībhirāhutaḥ ||
Ours art thou, Agni, Bhārata, honoured by us with barren cows,
With bullocks and with kine in calf
2.7.5
दर्वन्नःdrvannaḥ सर्पिरासुतिःsarpirāsutiḥ परत्नोpratno होताhotā वरेण्यःvareṇyaḥ |

सहससsahasas पुत्रोputro अद्भुतःadbhutaḥ ||
Wood-fed, bedewed with sacred oil, ancient, Invoker, excellent,
The Son of Strength, the Wonderful.
2.7.6
