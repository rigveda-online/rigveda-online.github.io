HYMN XIV. INDRA.

अध्वर्यवोadhvaryavo भरतेन्द्रायbharatendrāya सोममामत्रेभिःsomamāmatrebhiḥ सिञ्चताsiñcatā मद्यमन्धःmadyamandhaḥ |

कामीkāmī हिhi वीरःvīraḥ सदमस्यsadamasya पीतिंpītiṃ जुहोतjuhota वर्ष्णेvṛṣṇe तदिदेशtadideśa वष्टिvaṣṭi ||
MINISTERS, bring the Soma juice for Indra, pour forth the gladdening liquor with the beakers.
To drink of this the Hero longeth ever; offer it to the Bull, for this he willeth.
2.14.1
अध्वर्यवोadhvaryavo योyo अपोapo वव्रिवांसंvavrivāṃsaṃ वर्त्रंvṛtraṃ जघानाशन्येवjaghānāśanyeva वर्क्षमvṛkṣam |

तस्माtasmā एतंetaṃ भरतbharata तद्वशायनेषtadvaśāyaneṣa इन्द्रोindro अर्हतिarhati पीतिमस्यpītimasya ||
Ye ministers, to him who with the lightning smote, like a tree, the rain-withholding Vṛtra—
Bring it to him, him who is fain to taste it, a draught of this which Indra here deserveth.
2.14.2
अध्वर्यवोadhvaryavo योyo दर्भीकंdṛbhīkaṃ जघानjaghāna योyo गाghā उदाजदपudājadapa हिhi वलंvalaṃ वःvaḥ |

तस्माtasmā एतमन्तरिक्षेetamantarikṣe नna वातमिन्द्रंvātamindraṃ सोमैरोर्णुतsomairorṇuta जूर्नjūrna वस्त्रैःvastraiḥ ||
Ye ministers, to him who smote Dṛbhīka, who drove the kine forth, and discovered Vala,
Offer this draught, like Vita in the region: clothe him with Soma even as steeds with trappings.
2.14.3
अध्वर्यवोadhvaryavo यya उरणंuraṇaṃ जघानjaghāna नवnava चख्वांसंcakhvāṃsaṃ नवतिंnavatiṃ चबाहूनcabāhūn |

योyo अर्बुदमवarbudamava नीचाnīcā बबाधेbabādhe तमिन्द्रंtamindraṃ सोमस्यभ्र्थेsomasyabhṛthe हिनोतhinota ||
Him who did Uraṇa to death, Adhvaryus! though showing arms ninety-and-nine in number;
Who cast down headlong Arbuda and slew him,—speed ye that Indra to our offered Soma.
2.14.4
अध्वर्यवोadhvaryavo यःyaḥ सवश्नंsvaśnaṃ जघानjaghāna यःyaḥ शुष्णमशुषंśuṣṇamaśuṣaṃ योyo वयंसमvyaṃsam |

यःyaḥ पिप्रुंpipruṃ नमुचिंnamuciṃ योyo रुधिक्रांrudhikrāṃ तस्माtasmā इन्द्रायान्धसोindrāyāndhaso जुहोतjuhota ||
Ye ministers, to him who struck down Svaśna, and did to death Vyaṁsa and greedy Śuṣṇa,
And Rudhikrās and Namuci and Pipru,—to him, to Indra, pour ye forth libation.
2.14.5
अध्वर्यवोadhvaryavo यःyaḥ शतंśataṃ शम्बरस्यśambarasya पुरोpuro बिभेदाश्मनेवbibhedāśmaneva पूर्वीःpūrvīḥ |

योyo वर्चिनःvarcinaḥ शतमिन्द्रःśatamindraḥ सहस्रमपावपदsahasramapāvapad भरतासोममस्मैbharatāsomamasmai ||
Ye ministers, to him who as with thunder demolished Śambara's hundred ancient castles;
Who cast down Varcin's sons, a hundred thousand,—to him, to Indra, offer ye the Soma.
2.14.6
अध्वर्यवोadhvaryavo यःyaḥ शतमाśatamā सहस्रंsahasraṃ भूम्याbhūmyā उपस्थे.अवपज्जघन्वानupasthe.avapajjaghanvān |

कुत्सस्यायोरतिथिग्वस्यkutsasyāyoratithighvasya वीरानvīrān नयाव्र्णगnyāvṛṇagh भरताbharatā सोममस्मैsomamasmai ||
Ye ministers, to him who slew a hundred thousand, and cast them down upon earth's bosom;
Who quelled the valiant men of Atithigva, Kutsa, and Āyu,—bring to him the Soma.
2.14.7
अध्वर्यवोadhvaryavo यनyan नरःnaraḥ कामयाध्वेkāmayādhve शरुष्टीśruṣṭī वहन्तोvahanto नशथाnaśathā तदिन्द्रेtadindre |

गभस्तिपूतंghabhastipūtaṃ भरतbharata शरुतायेन्द्रायśrutāyendrāya सोमंsomaṃ यज्यवोyajyavo जुहोतjuhota ||
Ministers, men, whatever thing ye long for obtain ye quickly bringing gifts to Indra.
Bring to the Glorious One what bands have cleansed; to Indra bring, ye pious ones, the Soma.
2.14.8
अध्वर्यवःadhvaryavaḥ कर्तनाkartanā शरुष्टिमस्मैśruṣṭimasmai वनेvane निपूतंnipūtaṃ वनvana उनun नयध्वमnayadhvam |

जुषाणोjuṣāṇo हस्त्यमभिhastyamabhi वावशेvāvaśe वva इन्द्रायindrāya सोमंsomaṃ मदिरंmadiraṃ जुहोतjuhota ||
Do ye, O ministers, obey his order: that purified in wood, in wood uplift ye.
Well pleased he longs for what your hands have tended: offer the gladdening Soma juice to Indra.
2.14.9
अध्वर्यवःadhvaryavaḥ पयसोधर्यथाpayasodharyathā गोःghoḥ सोमेभिरींsomebhirīṃ पर्णताpṛṇatā भोजमिन्द्रमbhojamindram |

वेदाहमस्यvedāhamasya निभ्र्तंnibhṛtaṃ मma एतदetad दित्सन्तंditsantaṃ भूयोbhūyo यजतश्चिकेतyajataściketa ||
As the cow's udder teems with milk, Adhvaryus, so fill with Soma Indra, liberal giver.
I know him: I am sure of this, the Holy knows that I fain would give to him more largely.
2.14.10
अध्वर्यवोadhvaryavo योyo दिव्यस्यdivyasya वस्वोvasvo यःyaḥ पार्थिवस्यpārthivasya कषम्यस्यkṣamyasya राजाrājā |

तमूर्दरंtamūrdaraṃ नna परिणताpriṇatā यवेनेन्द्रंyavenendraṃ सोमेभिस्तदपोवोsomebhistadapovo अस्तुastu ||
Him, ministers, the Lord of heavenly treasure and all terrestrial wealth that earth possesses,
Him, Indra, fill with Soma as a garner is filled with barley full: be this your labour.
2.14.11
अस्मभ्यंasmabhyaṃ तदtad ...... ||
Prepare thyself to grant us that great booty, O Vasu, for abundant is thy treasure.
Gather up wondrous wealth, O Indra, daily. Loud may we speak, with heroes, in assembly.
