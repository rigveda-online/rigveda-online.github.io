HYMN XXVII. AGNI.

अनस्वन्ताanasvantā सत्पतिरsatpatir मामहेmāmahe मेme गावाghāvā चेतिष्ठोcetiṣṭho असुरोasuro मघोनःmaghonaḥ |

तरैव्र्ष्णोtraivṛṣṇo अग्नेaghne दशभिःdaśabhiḥ सहस्रैरsahasrair वैश्वानरvaiśvānara तर्यरुणशtryaruṇaś चिकेतciketa ||
THE Godlike hero, famousest of nobles, hath granted me two oxen with a wagon.
Trvrsan's son Tryaruna hath distinguished himself, Vaiśvānara Agni! with ten thousands.
5.27.1
योyo मेme शताśatā चca विंशतिंviṃśatiṃ चca गोनांghonāṃ हरीharī चca युक्ताyuktā सुधुराsudhurā ददातिdadāti |

वैश्वानरvaiśvānara सुष्टुतोsuṣṭuto वाव्र्धानोvāvṛdhāno ऽगने'ghne यछyacha तर्यरुणायtryaruṇāya शर्मśarma ||
Protect Tryaruna, as thou art waxing strong and art highly praised, Vaiśvānara Agni!
Who granteth me a hundred kine and twenty, and two bay horses, good at draught, and harnessed.
5.27.2
एवाevā तेte अग्नेaghne सुमतिंsumatiṃ चकानोcakāno नविष्ठायnaviṣṭhāya नवमंnavamaṃ तरसदस्युःtrasadasyuḥ |

योyo मेme गिरसghiras तुविजातस्यtuvijātasya पूर्वीरpūrvīr युक्तेनाभिyuktenābhi तर्यरुणोtryaruṇo गर्णातिghṛṇāti ||
So Trasadasyu served thee, God Most Youthful, craving thy favour for the ninth time, Agni;
Tryaruya who with attentive spirit accepteth many a song from me the mighty.
5.27.3
योyo मma इतिiti परवोचत्यpravocaty अश्वमेधायaśvamedhāya सूरयेsūraye |

दददdadad रचाṛcā सनिंsaniṃ यतेyate ददनdadan मेधामmedhām रतायतेṛtāyate ||
He who declares his wish to me, to Asvamedha, to the Prince,
Pays him who with his verse seeks gain, gives power to him who keeps the Law.
5.27.4
यस्यyasya माmā परुषाःparuṣāḥ शतमśatam उद्धर्षयन्त्यuddharṣayanty उक्षणःukṣaṇaḥ |

अश्वमेधस्यaśvamedhasya दानाःdānāḥ सोमाsomā इवiva तर्य्र्शिरःtryṛśiraḥ ||
From whom a hundred oxen, all of speckled hue, delight my heart,
The gifts of Asvamedha, like thrice-mingled draughts of Soma juice.
5.27.5
इन्द्राग्नीindrāghnī शतदाव्न्यśatadāvny अश्वमेधेaśvamedhe सुवीर्यमsuvīryam |

कषत्रंkṣatraṃ धारयतमdhārayatam बर्हदbṛhad दिविdivi सूर्यमsūryam इवाजरमivājaram ||
To Asvamedha who bestows a hundred gifts grant hero power,
O Indra-Agni! lofty rule like the unwasting Sun in heaven.
5.27.6
