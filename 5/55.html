HYMN LV. MARUTS.

परयज्यवोprayajyavo मरुतोmaruto भराजद्र्ष्टयोbhrājadṛṣṭayo बर्हदbṛhad वयोvayo दधिरेdadhire रुक्मवक्षसःrukmavakṣasaḥ |

ईयन्तेīyante अश्वैःaśvaiḥ सुयमेभिरsuyamebhir आशुभिःāśubhiḥ शुभंśubhaṃ यातामyātām अनुanu रथाrathā अव्र्त्सतavṛtsata ||
WITH gleaming lances, with their breasts adorned with gold, the Maruts, rushing onward, hold high power of life.
They hasten with swift steeds easy to be controlled. Their cars moved onward as they went to victory.
5.55.1
सवयंsvayaṃ दधिध्वेdadhidhve तविषींtaviṣīṃ यथाyathā विदvida बर्हनbṛhan महान्तmahānta उर्वियाurviyā विvi राजथrājatha |

उतान्तरिक्षमutāntarikṣam ममिरेmamire वयvy ओजसाojasā शुभंśubhaṃ यातामyātām अनुanu रथाrathā अव्र्त्सतavṛtsata ||
Ye, as ye wist, have gained of your own selves your power: high, O ye Mighty Ones, and wide ye shine abroad.
They with their strength have even measured out the sky.
Their cars moved onward as they went to victory.
5.55.2
साकंsākaṃ जाताःjātāḥ सुभ्वःsubhvaḥ साकमsākam उक्षिताःukṣitāḥ शरियेśriye चिदcid आā परतरंprataraṃ वाव्र्धुरvāvṛdhur नरःnaraḥ |

विरोकिणःvirokiṇaḥ सूर्यस्येवsūryasyeva रश्मयःraśmayaḥ शुभंśubhaṃ यातामyātām अनुanu रथाrathā अव्र्त्सतavṛtsata ||
Strong, born together, they together have waxed great: the Heroes more and more have grown to majesty
Resplendent as the Sun's beams in their light are they. Their cars moved onward as they went to victory.
5.55.3
आभूषेण्यंābhūṣeṇyaṃ वोvo मरुतोmaruto महित्वनंmahitvanaṃ दिद्र्क्षेण्यंdidṛkṣeṇyaṃ सूर्यस्येवsūryasyeva चक्षणमcakṣaṇam |

उतोuto अस्मांasmāṃ अम्र्तत्वेamṛtatve दधातनdadhātana शुभंśubhaṃ यातामyātām अनुanu रथाrathā अव्र्त्सतavṛtsata ||
Maruts, your mightiness deserves to be adored, sight to be longed for like the shining of the Sun.
So lead us with your aid to immortality.
Their cars moved onward as they went to victory.
5.55.4
उदud ईरयथाīrayathā मरुतःmarutaḥ समुद्रतोsamudrato यूयंyūyaṃ वर्ष्टिंvṛṣṭiṃ वर्षयथाvarṣayathā पुरीषिणःpurīṣiṇaḥ |

नna वोvo दस्राdasrā उपupa दस्यन्तिdasyanti धेनवःdhenavaḥ शुभंśubhaṃ यातामyātām अनुanu रथाrathā अव्र्त्सतavṛtsata ||
O Maruts, from the Ocean ye uplift the rain, and fraught with vaporous moisture pour the torrents down.
Never, ye Wonder-Workers, are your Milch-kine dry. Their cars moved onward as they went to victory.
5.55.5
यदyad अश्वानaśvān धूर्षुdhūrṣu पर्षतीरpṛṣatīr अयुग्ध्वंayughdhvaṃ हिरण्ययानhiraṇyayān परत्यpraty अत्कांatkāṃ अमुग्ध्वमamughdhvam |

विश्वाviśvā इतit सप्र्धोspṛdho मरुतोmaruto वयvy अस्यथasyatha शुभंśubhaṃ यातामyātām अनुanu रथाrathā अव्र्त्सतavṛtsata ||
When to your car-poles ye have yoked your spotted deer to be your steeds, and put your golden mantles on,
O Maruts, ye disperse all enemies abroad. Their cars moved onward as they went to victory.
5.55.6
नna पर्वताparvatā नna नद्योnadyo वरन्तvaranta वोvo यत्राचिध्वमyatrācidhvam मरुतोmaruto गछथेदghachathed उu ततtat |

उतuta दयावाप्र्थिवीdyāvāpṛthivī याथनाyāthanā परिpari शुभंśubhaṃ यातामyātām अनुanu रथाrathā अव्र्त्सतavṛtsata ||
Neither the mountains nor the rivers keep you back: whither ye have resolved thither ye, Maruts, go.
Ye compass round about even the heaven and earth. Their cars moved onward as they went to victory.
Whate’er is ancient, Maruts, what of recent time, whate’er is spoken, Vasus, what is chanted forth,
They who take cognizance of all of this are ye. Their cars moved onward as they went to victory.
5.55.7
यतyat पूर्व्यमpūrvyam मरुतोmaruto यचyac चca नूतनंnūtanaṃ यदyad उद्यतेudyate वसवोvasavo यचyac चca शस्यतेśasyate |

विश्वस्यviśvasya तस्यtasya भवथाbhavathā नवेदसःnavedasaḥ शुभंśubhaṃ यातामyātām अनुanu रथाrathā अव्र्त्सतavṛtsata ||
Be gracious unto us, ye Maruts, slay us not extend ye unto us shelter of many a sort.
Pay due regard unto our friendship and our praise. Their cars moved onward as they went to victory.
5.55.8
मर्ळतmṛḷata नोno मरुतोmaruto माmā वधिष्टनास्मभ्यंvadhiṣṭanāsmabhyaṃ शर्मśarma बहुलंbahulaṃ विvi यन्तनyantana |

अधिadhi सतोत्रस्यstotrasya सख्यस्यsakhyasya गातनghātana शुभंśubhaṃ यातामyātām अनुanu रथाrathā अव्र्त्सतavṛtsata ||
O Maruts, lead us on to higher fortune deliver us, when lauded, from afflictions.
Accept, ye Holy Ones, the gifts we bring you. May we be masters of abundant riches.
5.55.9
यूयमyūyam अस्मानasmān नयतnayata वस्योvasyo अछाachā निरnir अंहतिभ्योaṃhatibhyo मरुतोmaruto गर्णानाःghṛṇānāḥ |

जुषध्वंjuṣadhvaṃ नोno हव्यदातिंhavyadātiṃ यजत्राyajatrā वयंvayaṃ सयामsyāma पतयोpatayo रयीणामrayīṇām ||
