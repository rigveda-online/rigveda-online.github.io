HYMN LVII. MARUTS.

आā रुद्रासrudrāsa इन्द्रवन्तःindravantaḥ सजोषसोsajoṣaso हिरण्यरथाःhiraṇyarathāḥ सुवितायsuvitāya गन्तनghantana |

इयंiyaṃ वोvo अस्मतasmat परतिprati हर्यतेharyate मतिसmatis तर्ष्णजेtṛṣṇaje नna दिवdiva उत्साutsā उदन्यवेudanyave ||
OF one accord, with Indra, O ye Rudras, come borne on your golden car for our prosperity.
An offering from us, this hymn is brought to you, as, unto one who th irsts for water, heavenly springs.
5.57.1
वाशीमन्तvāśīmanta रष्टिमन्तोṛṣṭimanto मनीषिणःmanīṣiṇaḥ सुधन्वानsudhanvāna इषुमन्तोiṣumanto निषङगिणःniṣaṅghiṇaḥ |

सवश्वाsvaśvā सथstha सुरथाःsurathāḥ पर्श्निमातरःpṛśnimātaraḥ सवायुधाsvāyudhā मरुतोmaruto याथनाyāthanā शुभमśubham ||
Armed with your daggers, full of wisdom, armed with spears, armed with your quivers, armed with arrows, with good bows,
Good horses and good cars have ye, O Pṛśni's Sons: ye, Maruts, with good weapons go to victory.
5.57.2
धूनुथdhūnutha दयामdyām पर्वतानparvatān दाशुषेdāśuṣe वसुvasu निni वोvo वनाvanā जिहतेjihate यामनोyāmano भियाbhiyā |

कोपयथkopayatha पर्थिवीमpṛthivīm पर्श्निमातरःpṛśnimātaraḥ शुभेśubhe यदyad उग्राःughrāḥ पर्षतीरpṛṣatīr अयुग्ध्वमayughdhvam ||
From hills and heaven ye shake wealth for the worshipper: in terror at your coming low the woods bow down.
Ye make the earth to tremble, Sons of Pṛśni, when for victory ye have yoked, fierce Ones! your spotted deer.
5.57.3
वातत्विषोvātatviṣo मरुतोmaruto वर्षनिर्णिजोvarṣanirṇijo यमाyamā इवiva सुसद्र्शःsusadṛśaḥ सुपेशसःsupeśasaḥ |

पिशङगाश्वाpiśaṅghāśvā अरुणाश्वाaruṇāśvā अरेपसःarepasaḥ परत्वक्षसोpratvakṣaso महिनाmahinā दयौरdyaur इवोरवःivoravaḥ ||
Bright with the blasts of wind, wrapped in their robes of rain, like twins of noble aspect and of lovely form,
The Maruts, spotless, with steeds tawnyhued and red, strong in their mightiness and spreading wide like heaven.
5.57.4
पुरुद्रप्साpurudrapsā अञ्जिमन्तःañjimantaḥ सुदानवसsudānavas तवेषसंद्र्शोtveṣasaṃdṛśo अनवभ्रराधसःanavabhrarādhasaḥ |

सुजातासोsujātāso जनुषाjanuṣā रुक्मवक्षसोrukmavakṣaso दिवोdivo अर्काarkā अम्र्तंamṛtaṃ नामnāma भेजिरेbhejire ||
Rich in adornment, rich in drops, munificent, bright in their aspect, yielding bounties that endure,
Noble by birth, adorned with gold upon their breasts, the Singers of the sky have won immortal fame.
5.57.5
रष्टयोṛṣṭayo वोvo मरुतोmaruto अंसयोरaṃsayor अधिadhi सहsaha ओजोojo बाह्वोरbāhvor वोvo बलंbalaṃ हितमhitam |

नर्म्णाnṛmṇā शीर्षस्वśīrṣasv आयुधाāyudhā रथेषुratheṣu वोvo विश्वाviśvā वःvaḥ शरीरśrīr अधिadhi तनूषुtanūṣu पिपिशेpipiśe ||
Borne on both shoulders, O ye Maruts, are your spears: within your arms is laid your energy and 3trength.
Bold thoughts are in your heads, your weapons in your cars, all glorious majesty is moulded on your forms.
5.57.6
गोमदghomad अश्वावदaśvāvad रथवतrathavat सुवीरंsuvīraṃ चन्द्रवदcandravad राधोrādho मरुतोmaruto ददाdadā नःnaḥ |

परशस्तिंpraśastiṃ नःnaḥ कर्णुतkṛṇuta रुद्रियासोrudriyāso भक्षीयbhakṣīya वोvo ऽवसो'vaso दैव्यस्यdaivyasya ||
Vouchsafe to us, O Maruts, splendid bounty in cattle and in steeds, in cars and heroes.
Children of Rudra, give us high distinction: may I enjoy your Godlike help and favour.
5.57.7
हयेhaye नरोnaro मरुतोmaruto मर्ळताmṛḷatā नसnas तुवीमघासोtuvīmaghāso अम्र्ताamṛtā रतज्ञाःṛtajñāḥ |

सत्यश्रुतःsatyaśrutaḥ कवयोkavayo युवानोyuvāno बर्हद्गिरयोbṛhadghirayo बर्हदbṛhad उक्षमाणाःukṣamāṇāḥ ||
Ho! Maruts, Heroes, skilled in Law, immortal, be gracious unto us, ye rich in treasures,
Ye hearers of the truth, ye sage and youthful, grown mighty, dwelling on the lofty mountains.
