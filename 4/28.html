HYMN XXVIII. INDRA-SOMA.

तवाtvā युजाyujā तवtava ततtat सोमsoma सख्यsakhya इन्द्रोindro अपोapo मनवेmanave सस्रुतसsasrutas कःkaḥ |

अहन्नahann अहिमahim अरिणातariṇāt सप्तsapta सिन्धूनsindhūn अपाव्र्णोदapāvṛṇod अपिहितेवapihiteva खानिkhāni ||
ALLIED with thee, in this thy friendship, Soma, Indra for man made waters flow together,
Slew Ahi, and sent forth the Seven Rivers, and opened as it were obstructed fountains.
4.28.1
तवाtvā युजाyujā निni खिदतkhidat सूर्यस्येन्द्रशsūryasyendraś चक्रंcakraṃ सहसाsahasā सद्यsadya इन्दोindo |

अधिadhi षणुनाṣṇunā बर्हताbṛhatā वर्तमानमvartamānam महोmaho दरुहोdruho अपapa विश्वायुviśvāyu धायिdhāyi ||
Indu, with thee for his confederate, Indra swiftly with might pressed down the wheel of Sūrya.
What rolled, all life's support, on heaven's high summit was separated from the great oppressor.
4.28.2
अहन्नahann इन्द्रोindro अदहदadahad अग्निरaghnir इन्दोindo पुराpurā दस्यूनdasyūn मध्यंदिनादmadhyaṃdinād अभीकेabhīke |

दुर्गेdurghe दुरोणेduroṇe करत्वाkratvā नna यातामyātām पुरूpurū सहस्राsahasrā शर्वाśarvā निni बर्हीतbarhīt ||
Indra smote down, Agni consumed, O Indu, the Dasyus ere the noontide in the conflict.
Of those who gladly sought a hard-won dwelling he cast down many a thousand with his arrow.
4.28.3
विश्वस्मातviśvasmāt सीमsīm अधमांadhamāṃ इन्द्रindra दस्यूनdasyūn विशोviśo दासीरdāsīr अक्र्णोरakṛṇor अप्रशस्ताःapraśastāḥ |

अबाधेथामabādhethām अम्र्णतंamṛṇataṃ निni शत्रूनśatrūn अविन्देथामavindethām अपचितिंapacitiṃ वधत्रैःvadhatraiḥ ||
Lower than all besides hast thou, O Indra, cast down the Dasyus, abject tribes of Dāsas.
Ye drave away, ye put to death the foemen, and took great vengeance with your murdering weapons.
4.28.4
एवाevā सत्यमsatyam मघवानाmaghavānā युवंyuvaṃ तदtad इन्द्रशindraś चca सोमोर्वमsomorvam अश्व्यंaśvyaṃ गोःghoḥ |

आदर्द्र्तमādardṛtam अपिहितान्यapihitāny अश्नाaśnā रिरिचथुःriricathuḥ कषाशkṣāś चितcit तत्र्दानाtatṛdānā ||
So, of a truth, Indra and Soma, Heroes, ye burst the stable of the kine and horses,
The stable which the bar or stone obstructed; and piercing through set free the habitations.
