HYMN XLVII. INDRA.

मरुत्वानिन्द्रmarutvānindra वर्षभोvṛṣabho रणायraṇāya पिबाpibā सोममनुष्वधंsomamanuṣvadhaṃ मदायmadāya |

आā सिञ्चस्वsiñcasva जठरेjaṭhare मध्वmadhva ऊर्मिंūrmiṃ तवंtvaṃ राजासिrājāsi परदिवःpradivaḥ सुतानामsutānām ||
DRINK, Indra, Marut-girt, as Bull, the Soma, for joy, for rapture even as thou listest.
Pour down the flood of meath within thy belly: thou from of old art King of Soma juices.
3.47.1
सजोषाsajoṣā इन्द्रindra सगणोsaghaṇo मरुद्भिःmarudbhiḥ सोमंsomaṃ पिबpiba वर्त्रहाvṛtrahā शूरśūra विद्वानvidvān |

जहिjahi शत्रून्रपśatrūnrapa मर्धोmṛdho नुदस्वाथाभयंnudasvāthābhayaṃ कर्णुहिkṛṇuhi विश्वतोviśvato नःnaḥ ||
Indra, accordant, with the banded Maruts, drink Soma, Hero, as wise Vṛtra-slayer.
Slay thou our foemen, drive away assailants and make us safe on every side from danger.
3.47.2
उतuta रतुभिर्र्तुपाःṛtubhirṛtupāḥ पाहिpāhi सोममिन्द्रsomamindra देवेभिःdevebhiḥ सखिभिःsakhibhiḥ सुतंsutaṃ नःnaḥ |

यानाभजोyānābhajo मरुतोmaruto येye तवान्वहनtvānvahan वर्त्रमदधुस्तुभ्यमोजःvṛtramadadhustubhyamojaḥ ||
And, drinker at due seasons, drink in season, Indra, with friendly Gods, our pressed-out Soma.
The Maruts following, whom thou madest sharers, gave thee the victory, and thou slewest Vṛtra.
3.47.3
येye तवाहिहत्येtvāhihatye मघवन्नवर्धनmaghavannavardhan येye शाम्बरेśāmbare हरिवोharivo येye गविष्टौghaviṣṭau |

येye तवाtvā नूनमनुमदन्तिnūnamanumadanti विप्राःviprāḥ पिबेन्द्रpibendra सोमंsomaṃ सगणोsaghaṇo मरुद्भिःmarudbhiḥ ||
Drink Soma, Indra, banded with the Maruts who, Maghavan, strengthened thee at Ahi's slaughter,
'Gainst Śambara, Lord of Bays! in winning cattle, and now rejoice in thee, the holy Singers.
3.47.4
मरुत्वन्तंmarutvantaṃ वर्षभंvṛṣabhaṃ वाव्र्धानमकवारिंvāvṛdhānamakavāriṃ दिव्यंdivyaṃ शासमिन्द्रमśāsamindram |

विश्वासाहमवसेviśvāsāhamavase नूतनायोग्रंnūtanāyoghraṃ सहोदामिहsahodāmiha तंहुवेमtaṃhuvema ||
The Bull whose strength hath waxed, whom Maruts follow, free-giving Indra, the celestial Ruler,
Mighty, all-conquering, the victory-giver, him let us call to grant us new protection.
3.47.5
