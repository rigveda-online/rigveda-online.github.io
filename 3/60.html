HYMN LX. ṚBHUS.

इहेहiheha वोvo मनसाmanasā बन्धुताbandhutā नरnara उशिजोuśijo जग्मुरभिjaghmurabhi तानिtāni वेदसाvedasā |

याभिर्मायाभिःyābhirmāyābhiḥ परतिजूतिवर्पसःpratijūtivarpasaḥ सौधन्वनाsaudhanvanā यज्ञियंyajñiyaṃ भागमानशbhāghamānaśa ||
HERE is your ghostly kinship, here, O Men: they came desirous to these holy rites with store of wealth,
With wondrous arts, whereby, with schemes to meet each need, Ye gained, Sudhanvan's Sons! your share in sacrifice.
3.60.1
याभिःyābhiḥ शचीभिश्चमसानपिंशतśacībhiścamasānapiṃśata ययाyayā धियाdhiyā गामरिणीतghāmariṇīta चर्मणःcarmaṇaḥ |

येनyena हरीharī मनसाmanasā निरतक्षतniratakṣata तेनtena देवत्वंdevatvaṃ रभवःṛbhavaḥ समानशsamānaśa ||
The mighty powers wherewith. ye formed the chalices, the thought by which ye drew the cow from out the hide,
The intellect wherewith ye wrought the two Bay Steeds,—through these, O Ṛbhus, ye attained divinity.
3.60.2
इन्द्रस्यindrasya सख्यंsakhyaṃ रभवःṛbhavaḥ समानशुर्मनोर्नपातोsamānaśurmanornapāto अपसोapaso दधन्विरेdadhanvire |

सौधन्वनासोsaudhanvanāso अम्र्तत्वमेरिरेamṛtatvamerire विष्ट्वीviṣṭvī शमीभिःśamībhiḥ सुक्र्तःsukṛtaḥ सुक्र्त्ययाsukṛtyayā ||
Friendship with Indra have the Ṛbhus, fully gained: grandsons of Manu, they skilfully urged the work.
Sudhanvan's Children won them everlasting life, serving with holy rites, pious with noble acts.
4:In company with Indra come ye to the juice, then gloriously shall your wishes be fulfilled.
Not to be paragoned, ye Priests, are your good deeds, nor your heroic acts, Ṛbhus, Sudhanvan's Sons.
3.60.3
इन्द्रेणindreṇa याथyātha सरथंsarathaṃ सुतेsute सचानथोsacānatho वशानांvaśānāṃ भवथासहbhavathāsaha शरियाśriyā |

नna वःvaḥ परतिमैpratimai सुक्र्तानिsukṛtāni वाघतःvāghataḥ सौधन्वनाsaudhanvanā रभवोṛbhavo वीर्याणिvīryāṇi चca ||
O Indra, with the Ṛbhus, Mighty Ones, pour down the Soma juice effused, well-blent, from both thy hands.
Maghalan, urged by song, in the drink-offerer's house rejoice thee with the Heroes, with Sudhanvan's Sons.
3.60.4
इन्द्रindra रभुभिर्वाजवद्भिःṛbhubhirvājavadbhiḥ समुक्षितंsamukṣitaṃ सुतंsutaṃ सोममाsomamā वर्षस्वाvṛṣasvā गभस्त्योःghabhastyoḥ |

धियेषितोdhiyeṣito मघवनmaghavan दाशुषोdāśuṣo गर्हेghṛhe सौधन्वनेभिःsaudhanvanebhiḥ सहsaha मत्स्वाmatsvā नर्भिःnṛbhiḥ ||
With Ṛbhu near, and Vāja, Indra, here exult, with Saci, praised of many, in the juice we pour.
These homes wherein we dwell have turned themselves to thee, -devotions to the Gods, as laws of men ordain.
3.60.5
इन्द्रindra रभुमानṛbhumān वाजवानvājavān मत्स्वेहmatsveha नो.अस्मिनno.asmin सवनेsavane शच्याśacyā पुरुष्टुतpuruṣṭuta |

इमानिimāni तुभ्यंtubhyaṃ सवसराणिsvasarāṇi येमिरेyemire वरताvratā देवानांdevānāṃ मनुषश्चmanuṣaśca धर्मभिःdharmabhiḥ ||
Come with the mighty Ṛbhus, Indra, come to us, strengthening with thy help the singer's holy praise;
At hundred eager calls come to the living man, with thousand arts attend the act of sacrifice.
3.60.6
इन्द्रindra रभुभिर्वाजिभिर्वाजयन्निहṛbhubhirvājibhirvājayanniha सतोमंstomaṃ जरितुरुपjariturupa याहिyāhi यज्ञियमyajñiyam |

शतंśataṃ केतेभिरिषिरेभिरायवेketebhiriṣirebhirāyave सहस्रणीथोध्वरस्यsahasraṇīthoadhvarasya होमनिhomani ||
