HYMN XV. AGNI.

विvi पाजसाpājasā पर्थुनाpṛthunā शोशुचानोśośucāno बाधस्वbādhasva दविषोdviṣo रक्षसोrakṣaso अमीवाःamīvāḥ |

सुशर्मणोsuśarmaṇo बर्हतःbṛhataḥ शर्मणिśarmaṇi सयामग्नेरहंsyāmaghnerahaṃ सुहवस्यsuhavasya परणीतौpraṇītau ||
RESPLENDENT with thy wide-extending lustre, dispel the terrors of the fiends who hate us
May lofty Agni be my guide and shelter, the easily-invoked, the good Protector.
3.15.1
तवंtvaṃ नोno अस्याasyā उषसोuṣaso वयुष्टौvyuṣṭau तवंtvaṃ सूरsūra उदितेudite बोधिbodhi गोपाःghopāḥ |

जन्मेवjanmeva नित्यंnityaṃ तनयंtanayaṃ जुषस्वjuṣasva सतोमंstomaṃ मेme अग्नेaghne तन्वाtanvā सुजातsujāta ||
Be thou To us, while now the morn is breaking, be thou a guardian when the Sun hath mounted..
Accept, as men accept a true-born infant, my laud, O Agni nobly born in body.
3.15.2
तवंtvaṃ नर्चक्षाnṛcakṣā वर्षभानुvṛṣabhānu पूर्वीःpūrvīḥ कर्ष्णास्वग्नेkṛṣṇāsvaghne अरुषोaruṣo विभाहिvibhāhi |

वसोvaso नेषिneṣi चca पर्षिparṣi चात्यंहःcātyaṃhaḥ कर्धीkṛdhī नोno रायrāya उशिजोuśijo यविष्ठyaviṣṭha ||
Bull, who beholdest men, through many mornings, among the dark ones shine forth red, O Agni.
Lead us, good Lord, and bear us over trouble: Help us who long, Most Youthful God, to riches.
3.15.3
अषाळ्होaṣāḷho अग्नेaghne वर्षभोvṛṣabho दिदीहिdidīhi पुरोpuro विश्वाःviśvāḥ सौभगाsaubhaghā संजिगीवानsaṃjighīvān |

यज्ञस्यyajñasya नेताnetā परथमस्यprathamasya पायोर्जातवेदोpāyorjātavedo बर्हतःbṛhataḥ सुप्रणीतेsupraṇīte ||
Shine forth, a Bull invincible, O Agni, winning by conquest all the forts and treasures,
Thou Jātavedas who art skilled in guiding, the chief high saving sacrifice's Leader.
3.15.4
अछिद्राachidrā शर्मśarma जरितःjaritaḥ पुरूणिpurūṇi देवानछाdevānachā दीद्यानःdīdyānaḥ सुमेधाःsumedhāḥ |

रथोratho नna सस्निरभिsasnirabhi वक्षिvakṣi वाजमगनेvājamaghne तवंtvaṃ रोदसीनःrodasīnaḥ सुमेकेsumeke ||
Lighting Gods hither, Agni, wisest Singer, bring thou to us many and flawless shelters.
Bring vigour, like a car that gathers booty: bring us, O Agni, beauteous Earth and Heaven.
3.15.5
परpra पीपयpīpaya वर्षभvṛṣabha जिन्वjinva वाजानग्नेvājānaghne तवंtvaṃ रोदसीrodasī नःnaḥ सुदोघेsudoghe |

देवेभिर्देवdevebhirdeva सुरुचाsurucā रुचानोrucāno माmā नोno मर्तस्यmartasya दुर्मतिःdurmatiḥ परिpari षठातṣṭhāt ||
Swell, O thou Bull and give those powers an impulse, e’en Earth and Heaven who yield their milk in plenty,
Shining, O God, with Gods in clear effulgence. Let not a mortal's evil will obstruct us.
3.15.6
इळामग्नेiḷāmaghne ...... ||
Agni, as holy food to thine invoker, give wealth in cattle, lasting, rich in marvels.
To us be born a son and spreading offspring. Agni, be this thy gracious will to us-ward.
3.15.7
