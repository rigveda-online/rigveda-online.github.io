HYMN XLVI. INDRA.

युध्मस्यyudhmasya तेte वर्षभस्यvṛṣabhasya सवराजsvarāja उग्रस्यughrasya यूनyūna सथविरस्यsthavirasya घर्ष्वेःghṛṣveḥ |

अजूर्यतोajūryato वज्रिणोvajriṇo वीर्याणीन्द्रvīryāṇīndra शरुतस्यśrutasya महतोmahato महानिmahāni ||
OF thee, the Bull, the Warrior, Sovran Ruler, joyous and fierce, ancient and ever youthful,
The undecaying One who wields the thunder, renowned and great, great are the exploits, Indra.
3.46.1
महानसिmahānasi महिषmahiṣa वर्ष्ण्येभिर्धनस्प्र्दुग्रvṛṣṇyebhirdhanaspṛdughra सहमानोsahamāno अन्यानanyān |

एकोeko विश्वस्यviśvasya भुवनस्यbhuvanasya राजाrājā सsa योधयाyodhayā चca कषययाkṣayayā चca जनानjanān ||
Great art thou, Mighty Lord, through manly vigour, O fierce One, gathering spoil, subduing others,
Thyself alone the universe's Sovran: so send forth men to combat and to rest them.
3.46.2
परpra मात्राभीmātrābhī रिरिचेririce रोचमानःrocamānaḥ परpra देवेभिर्विश्वतोdevebhirviśvato अप्रतीतःapratītaḥ |

परpra मज्मनाmajmanā दिवdiva इन्द्रःindraḥ पर्थिव्याःpṛthivyāḥ परोरोर्महोprorormaho अन्तरिक्षादantarikṣād रजीषीṛjīṣī ||
He hath surpassed all measure in his brightness, yea, and the Gods, for none may be his equal.
Impetuous Indra in his might cxccedcth wide vast mid-air and heaven and earth together.
3.46.3
उरुंuruṃ गभीरंghabhīraṃ जनुषाभ्युग्रंjanuṣābhyughraṃ विश्वव्यचसमवतंviśvavyacasamavataṃ मतीनामmatīnām |

इन्द्रंindraṃ सोमासःsomāsaḥ परदिविpradivi सुतासःsutāsaḥ समुद्रंsamudraṃ नna सरवताsravataā विशन्तिviśanti ||
To Indra, even as rivers to the ocean, flow forth from days of old the Soma juices;
To him wide deep and mighty from his birth-time, the well of holy thoughts, aIl-comprehending.
3.46.4
यंyaṃ सोममिन्द्रsomamindra पर्थिवीद्यावाpṛthivīdyāvā गर्भंgharbhaṃ नna माताmātā बिभ्र्तस्त्वायाbibhṛtastvāyā |

तंtaṃ तेte हिन्वन्तिhinvanti तमुtamu तेte मर्जन्त्यध्वर्यवोmṛjantyadhvaryavo वर्षभvṛṣabha पातवाpātavā उu ||
The Soma, Indra, which the earth and heaven bear for thee as a mother bears her infant,
This they send forth to thee, this, vigorous Hero! Adhvaryus purify for thee to drink of.
3.46.5
