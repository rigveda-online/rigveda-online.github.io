HYMN XXIII. AGNI.

निर्मथितःnirmathitaḥ सुधितsudhita आā सधस्थेsadhasthe युवाyuvā कविरध्वरस्यkaviradhvarasya परणेताpraṇetā |

जूर्यत्स्वग्निरजरोjūryatsvaghnirajaro वनेष्वत्राvaneṣvatrā दधेdadhe अम्र्तंamṛtaṃ जातवेदाःjātavedāḥ ||
RUBBED into life, well stablished in the dwelling, Leader of sacrifice, the Sage, the youthful,
Here in the wasting fuel Jātavedas, eternal, hath assumed immortal being.
3.23.1
अमन्थिष्टांamanthiṣṭāṃ भारताbhāratā रेवदग्निंrevadaghniṃ देवश्रवाdevaśravā देववातःdevavātaḥ सुदक्षमsudakṣam |

अग्नेaghne विvi पश्यpaśya बर्हताभिbṛhatābhi रायेषांrāyeṣāṃ नोno नेताnetā भवतादनुbhavatādanu दयूनdyūn ||
Both Bharatas, Devaśravas, Devāvata, have strongly rubbed to life effectual Agni.
O Agni, look thou forth with ample riches: be, every day, bearer of food to feed us.
3.23.2
दशdaśa कषिपःkṣipaḥ पूर्व्यंpūrvyaṃ सीमजीजननsīmajījanan सुजातंsujātaṃ मात्र्षुmātṛṣu परियमpriyam |

अग्निंaghniṃ सतुहिstuhi दैववातंdaivavātaṃ देवश्रवोdevaśravo योyo जनानामसदjanānāmasad वशीvaśī ||
Him nobly born of old the fingers ten produced, him whom his Mothers counted dear.
Praise Devāvata's Agni, thou Devaśravas, him who shall be the people's Lord.
3.23.3
निni तवाtvā दधेdadhe वरvara आā पर्थिव्याpṛthivyā इळायासiḷāyās पदेpade सुदिनत्वेsudinatve अह्नामahnām |

दर्षद्वत्यांdṛṣadvatyāṃ मानुषmānuṣa आपयायांāpayāyāṃ सरस्वत्यांsarasvatyāṃ रेवदग्नेदिदीहिrevadaghnedidīhi ||
He set thee in the earth's most lovely station, in Iḷā's place, in days of fair bright weather.
On man, on Āpayā, Agni! on the rivers Dṛṣadvati, Sarasvatī, shine richly.
3.23.4
इळामग्नेiḷāmaghne ...... ||
Agni, as holy food to thine invoker give wealth in cattle, lasting, rich in marvels.
To us be born a son and spreading offspring Agni, be this thy gracious will to us-ward
