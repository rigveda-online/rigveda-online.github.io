HYMN XXXV. AŚVINS.

अग्निनेन्द्रेणaghninendreṇa वरुणेनvaruṇena विष्णुनादित्यैviṣṇunādityai रुद्रैर्वसुभिःrudrairvasubhiḥ सचाभुवाsacābhuvā |

सजोषसाsajoṣasā उषसाuṣasā सूर्येणsūryeṇa चca सोमंsomaṃ पिबतमश्विनाpibatamaśvinā ||
WITH Agni and with Indra, Viṣṇu. Varuṇa, with the Ādityas, Rudras, Vasus, closely leagued;
Accordant, of one mind with Sūrya and with Dawn, O Aśvins, drink the Soma juice.
8.35.1
विश्वाभिर्धीभिर्भुवनेनviśvābhirdhībhirbhuvanena वाजिनाvājinā दिवाdivā पर्थिव्याद्रिभिःpṛthivyādribhiḥ सचाभुवाsacābhuvā |

सजोषसाsajoṣasā उषसाuṣasā ...... ||
With all the Holy Thoughts, all being Mighty Ones! in close alliance wil the Mountains, Heaven, and Earth;
Accordant. of one mind with Sūrya and with Dawn, O Aśvins, drink the Soma juice.
8.35.2
विश्वैर्देवैस्त्रिभिरेकादशैरिहाद्भिर्मरुद्भिर्भ्र्गुभिःviśvairdevaistribhirekādaśairihādbhirmarudbhirbhṛghubhiḥ सचाभुवाsacābhuvā |

सजोषसाsajoṣasā उषसाuṣasā ...... ||
With all the Deities, three times eleven, here, in close alliance with the Maruts, Bhṛgus, Floods;
Accordant, of one mind with Sūrya and with Dawn, O Aśvins, drink the Soma juice.
8.35.3
जुषेथांjuṣethāṃ यज्ञंyajñaṃ बोधतंbodhataṃ हवस्यhavasya मेme विश्वेहviśveha देवौdevau सवनावsavanāva गछतमghachatam |

सजोषसाsajoṣasā उषसाuṣasā सूर्येणsūryeṇa चेषंceṣaṃ नोno वोळ्हमश्विनाvoḷhamaśvinā ||
Accept the sacrifice, attend to this my call: come nigh, O ye Twain Gods, to all libations here.
Accordant, of one mind with Sūrya and with Dawn, O Aśvins, bring us strengthening food.
8.35.4
सतोमंstomaṃ जुषेथांjuṣethāṃ युवशेवyuvaśeva कन्यनांkanyanāṃ विश्वेहviśveha देवौdevau सवनावsavanāva गछतमghachatam |

सजोषसाsajoṣasā उषसाuṣasā सूर्येनsūryena चेषंceṣaṃ ...... ||
Accept our praise-song as a youth accepts a maid. Come nigh, O ye Twain Gods, to all libations here.
Accordant, of one mind with Sūrya and with Dawn, O Aśvins, bring us strengthening food.
8.35.5
गिरोghiro जुषेथामध्वरंjuṣethāmadhvaraṃ जुषेथांjuṣethāṃ विश्वेहviśveha देवौdevau सवनावsavanāva गछतमghachatam |

सजोषसाsajoṣasā उषसाuṣasā सूर्येणsūryeṇa चेषंceṣaṃ ...... ||
Accept the songs we sing, accept the solemn rite. Come nigh, O ye Twain Gods, to all libations here.
Accordant, of one mind with Sūrya and with Dawn, O Aśvins, bring us strengthening food.
8.35.6
हारिद्रवेवhāridraveva पतथोpatatho वनेदुपvanedupa सोमंsomaṃ सुतंsutaṃ महिषेवावmahiṣevāva गछथःghachathaḥ |

सजोषसाsajoṣasā उषसाuṣasā सूर्येणsūryeṇa चca तरिर्वर्तिर्यातमश्विनाtrirvartiryātamaśvinā ||
Ye fly as starlings fly unto the forest trees; like buffaloes ye seek the Soma we have shed.
Accordant, of one mind with Sūrya and with Dawn, come thrice, O Aśvins, to our home.
8.35.7
हंसाविवhaṃsāviva पतथोpatatho अध्वगाविवadhvaghāviva सोमंsomaṃ सुतंsutaṃ महिषेवावmahiṣevāva गछथःghachathaḥ |

सजोषसाsajoṣasā उषसाuṣasā सूर्येणsūryeṇa चca तरिर...trir... ||
Ye fly like swans, like those who travel on their way; like buffaloes ye seek the Soma we have shed.
Accordant, of one mind with Sūrya and with Dawn, come thrice, O Aśvins, to our home.
8.35.8
शयेनाविवśyenāviva पतथोpatatho हव्यदातयेhavyadātaye सोमंsomaṃ सुतंsutaṃ महिषेवावmahiṣevāva गछथःghachathaḥ |

सजोषसाsajoṣasā उषसाuṣasā सूर्येणsūryeṇa चca तरिर...trir... ||
Ye fly to our oblation like a pair of hawks; like buffaloes ye seek the Soma we have shed.
Accordant, of one mind with Sūrya and with Dawn, come thrice, O Aśvins, to our home.
8.35.9
पिबतंpibataṃ चca तर्प्णुतंtṛpṇutaṃ चाcā चca गछतंghachataṃ परजांprajāṃ चca धत्तंdhattaṃ दरविणंdraviṇaṃ चca धत्तमdhattam |

सजोषसाsajoṣasā उषसाuṣasā सूर्येणsūryeṇa चोर्जंcorjaṃ नोno धत्तमश्विनाdhattamaśvinā ||
Come hitherward and drink and satisfy yourselves, bestow upon us progeny and affluence.
Accordant, of one mind with Sūrya and with Dawn, O Aśvins, grant us vigorous strength.
8.35.10
जयतंjayataṃ चca परpra सतुतंstutaṃ चca परpra चावतंcāvataṃ परजांprajāṃ चca धत्तंdhattaṃ दरविणंdraviṇaṃ चca धत्तमdhattam |

सजोषसाsajoṣasā उषसाuṣasā सूर्येणsūryeṇa चोर्जंcorjaṃ ...... ||
Conquer your foes, protect us, praise your worshippers; bestow upon us progeny and affluence.
Accordant, of one mind with Sūrya and with Dawn, O Aśvins, grant us vigorous strength.
8.35.11
हतंhataṃ चca शत्रूनśatrūn यततंyatataṃ चca मित्रिणःmitriṇaḥ परजांprajāṃ चca धत्तंdhattaṃ दरविणंdraviṇaṃ चca धत्तमdhattam |

सजोषसाsajoṣasā उषसाuṣasā सूर्येणsūryeṇa चोर्जंcorjaṃ ...... ||
Slay enemies, animate men whom ye befriend; bestow upon us progeny and affluence.
Accordant, of one mind with Sūrya and with Dawn, O Aśvins, grant us vigorous strength.
8.35.12
मित्रावरुणवन्ताmitrāvaruṇavantā उतuta धर्मवन्ताdharmavantā मरुत्वन्ताmarutvantā जरितुर्गछथोjariturghachatho हवमhavam |

सजोषसाsajoṣasā उषसाuṣasā सूर्येणsūryeṇa चादित्यैर्यातमश्विनाcādityairyātamaśvinā ||
With Mitra, Varuṇa, Dharma, and the Maruts in your company approach unto your praiser's call.
Accordant, of one mind with Sūrya and with Dawn, and with the Ādityas, Aśvins! come.
8.35.13
अङगिरस्वन्ताaṅghirasvantā उतuta विष्णुवन्ताviṣṇuvantā मरुत्वन्ताmarutvantā जरितुर्गछथोjariturghachatho हवमhavam |

सजोषसाsajoṣasā उषसाuṣasā सूर्येणsūryeṇa चादित्यैर...cādityair... ||
With Viṣṇu and the Aṅgirases attending you, and with the Maruts come unto your praiser's call.
Accordant, of one mind with Sūrya and with Dawn, and with the Ādityas, Aśvins! come.
8.35.14
रभुमन्ताṛbhumantā वर्षणाvṛṣaṇā वाजवन्ताvājavantā मरुत्वन्ताmarutvantā जरितुर्गछथोjariturghachatho हवमhavam |

सजोषसाsajoṣasā उषसाuṣasā सूर्येणsūryeṇa चादित्यैर...cādityair... ||
With Ṛbhus and With Vājas. O ye Mighty Ones, leagued with the Maruts come ye to your praiser's call.
Accordant, of one mind with Sūrya and with Dawn, and with the Ādityas, Aśvins! come.
8.35.15
बरह्मbrahma जिन्वतमुतjinvatamuta जिन्वतंjinvataṃ धियोdhiyo हतंhataṃ रक्षांसिrakṣāṃsi सेधतममीवाःsedhatamamīvāḥ |

सजोषसाsajoṣasā उषसाuṣasā सूर्येणsūryeṇa चca सोमंsomaṃ सुन्वतोsunvato अश्विनाaśvinā ||
Give spirit to our prayer and animate our thoughts; slay ye the Rākṣasas and drive away disease.
Accordant, of One mind with Sūrya and with Dawn, -the presser's Soma, Aśvins drink.
8.35.16
कषत्रंkṣatraṃ जिन्वतमुतjinvatamuta जिन्वतंjinvataṃ नॄनnṝn हतंhataṃ रक्षाण्सिrakṣāṇsi सेधतममीवाःsedhatamamīvāḥ |

सजोषसाsajoṣasā उषसाuṣasā सूर्येणsūryeṇa चca सोमंsomaṃ ...... ||
Strengthen the Ruling Power, strengthen the men of war; slay ye the Rākṣasas and drive away disease.
Accordant, of one mind with Sūrya and with Dawn, the presser's Soma, Aśvins drink.
8.35.17
धेनूर्जिन्वतमुतdhenūrjinvatamuta जिन्वतंjinvataṃ विशोviśo हतंhataṃ रक्षांसिrakṣāṃsi सेधतममीवाःsedhatamamīvāḥ |

सजोषसाsajoṣasā उषसाuṣasā सूर्येणsūryeṇa चca सोमंsomaṃ ...... ||
Give strength unto the milch-kine, give the people strength, slay ye the Rākṣasas and drive away disease.
Accordant, of one mind with Sūrya and with Dawn, the presser's Soma, Aśvins drink.
8.35.18
अत्रेरिवatreriva शर्णुतंśṛṇutaṃ पूर्व्यस्तुतिंpūrvyastutiṃ शयावाश्वस्यśyāvāśvasya सुन्वतोsunvato मदच्युताmadacyutā |

सजोषसाsajoṣasā उषसाuṣasā सूर्येनsūryena चाश्विनाcāśvinā तिरोह्न्यमtiroahnyam ||
As ye heard Atri's earliest eulogy, so hear Śyāvāśva, Soma-presser, ye who reel in joy.
Accordant, of one mind with Sūrya and with Dawn, drink juice, O Aśvins, three days old.
8.35.19
सर्गानिवsarghāniva सर्जतंsṛjataṃ सुष्टुतीरुपsuṣṭutīrupa शयावाश्वस्यśyāvāśvasya सुन्वतोsunvato मदच्युताmadacyutā |

सजोषसाsajoṣasā उषसाuṣasā सूर्येणsūryeṇa चाश्विनाcāśvinā ||
Further like running streams Śyāvāśva's eulogies who presses out the Soma, ye who reel in joy.
Accordant, of one mind with Sūrya and with Dawn, drink juice, O Aśvins, three days old.
8.35.20
रश्मीन्रिवraśmīnriva यछतमध्वरानुपyachatamadhvarānupa शयावाश्वस्यśyāvāśvasya सुन्वतोsunvato मदच्युताmadacyutā |

सजोषसाsajoṣasā उषसाuṣasā सूर्येणsūryeṇa चाश्विनाcāśvinā ...... ||
Seize, as ye grasp the reins, Śyāvāśva's solemn rites who presses out the Soma, ye who reel in joy.
Accordant, of one mind with Sūrya and with Dawn, drink juice, O Aśvins, three days old.
8.35.21
अर्वागarvāgh रथंrathaṃ निni यछतंyachataṃ पिबतंpibataṃ सोम्यंsomyaṃ मधुmadhu |

आā यातमश्विनाyātamaśvinā गतमवस्युर्वामहंghatamavasyurvāmahaṃ हुवेhuve धत्तंdhattaṃ रत्नानिratnāni दाशुषेdāśuṣe ||
Drive down your chariot hitherward drink ye the Soma's savoury juice.
Approach, ye Aśvins, come to us: I call you, eager for your aid. Grant treasures to the worshipper.
8.35.22
नमोवाकेnamovāke परस्थितेprasthite अध्वरेadhvare नराnarā विवक्षणस्यvivakṣaṇasya पीतयेpītaye |

आā यातंyātaṃ ...... ||
When sacrifice which tells our reverence hath begun. Heroes! to drink the gushing juice,
Approach, ye Aśvins, come to us: I call you, eager for your aid. Grant treasures to the worshipper.
8.35.23
सवाहाक्र्तस्यsvāhākṛtasya तर्म्पतंtṛmpataṃ सुतस्यsutasya देवावन्धसःdevāvandhasaḥ |

आā यातंyātaṃ ...... ||
Sate you with consecrated drink, with juice effused, ye Deities.
Approach, ye Aśvins, come to us: I call you, eager for your aid. Grant treasures to the worshipper.
8.35.24
