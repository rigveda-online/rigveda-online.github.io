HYMN CI. INDRA.

परpra मन्दिनेmandine पितुमदर्चताpitumadarcatā वचोvaco यःyaḥ कर्ष्णगर्भाkṛṣṇagharbhā निरहन्न्र्जिश्वनाnirahannṛjiśvanā |

अवस्यवोavasyavo वर्षणंvṛṣaṇaṃ वज्रदक्षिणंvajradakṣiṇaṃ मरुत्वन्तंmarutvantaṃ सख्यायsakhyāya हवामहेhavāmahe ||
SING, with oblation, praise to him who maketh glad, who with Ṛjiśvan drove the dusky brood away.
Fain for help, him the strong whose right hand wields the bolt, him girt by Maruts we invoke to be our Friend.
1.101.1
योyo वयंसंvyaṃsaṃ जाह्र्षाणेनjāhṛṣāṇena मन्युनाmanyunā यःyaḥ शम्बरंśambaraṃ योyo अहनahan पिप्रुमव्रतमpiprumavratam |

इन्द्रोindro यःyaḥ शुष्णमशुषंśuṣṇamaśuṣaṃ नयाव्र्णंnyāvṛṇaṃ म.ma. .... ||
Indra, who with triumphant wrath smote Vyaṁsa down, and Śambara, and Pipru the unrighteous one;
Who extirpated Śuṣṇa the insatiate,—him girt by Maruts we invoke to be our Friend.
1.101.2
यस्यyasya दयावाप्र्थिवीdyāvāpṛthivī पौंस्यंpauṃsyaṃ महदmahad यस्यyasya वरतेvrate वरुणोvaruṇo यस्यyasya सूर्यःsūryaḥ |

यस्येन्द्रस्यyasyendrasya सिन्धवःsindhavaḥ सश्चतिsaścati वरतंvrataṃ म...ma... ||
He whose great work of manly might is heaven and earth, and Varuṇa and Sūrya keep his holy law;
Indra, whose law the rivers follow as they flow,—him girt by Maruts we invoke to be our Friend.
1.101.3
योyo अश्वानांaśvānāṃ योyo गवांghavāṃ गोपतिर्वशीghopatirvaśī यya आरितःāritaḥ कर्मणिkarmaṇi कर्मणिkarmaṇi सथिरःsthiraḥ |

वीळोश्चिदिन्द्रोvīḷościdindro योyo असुन्वतोasunvato वधोvadho म...ma... ||
He who is Lord and Master of the steeds and kine, honoured—the firm and sure—at every holy act;
Stayer even of the strong who pours no offering out,—him girt by Maruts we invoke to be our Friend.
1.101.4
योyo विश्वस्यviśvasya जगतःjaghataḥ पराणतसprāṇatas पतिर्योpatiryo बरह्मणेbrahmaṇe परथमोprathamo गाghā अविन्दतavindat |

इन्द्रोindro योyo दस्यून्रधरानवातिरनdasyūnradharānavātiran म...ma... ||
He who is Lord of all the world that moves and breathes, who for the Brahman first before all found the Cows;
Indra who cast the Dasyus down beneath his feet,—him girt by Maruts we invoke to be our Friend.
1.101.5
यःyaḥ शूरेभिर्हव्योśūrebhirhavyo यश्चyaśca भीरुभिर्योbhīrubhiryo धावद्भिर्हूयतेdhāvadbhirhūyate यश्चyaśca जिग्युभिःjighyubhiḥ |

इन्द्रंindraṃ यंyaṃ विश्वाviśvā भुवनाभिbhuvanābhi सन्दधुर्म...sandadhurma... ||
Whom cowards must invoke and valiant men of war, invoked by those who conquer and by those who flee;
Indra, to whom all beings turn their constant thought,—him girt by Maruts we invoke to be our Friend.
1.101.6
रुद्राणामेतिrudrāṇāmeti परदिशाpradiśā विचक्षणोvicakṣaṇo रुद्रेभिर्योषाrudrebhiryoṣā तनुतेtanute पर्थुpṛthu जरयःjrayaḥ |

इन्द्रंindraṃ मनीषाmanīṣā अभ्यर्चतिabhyarcati शरुतंśrutaṃ म...ma... ||
Refulgent in the Rudras’ region he proceeds, and with the Rudras through the wide space speeds the Dame.
The hymn of praise extols Indra the far-renowned: him girt by Maruts we invoke to be our Friend.
1.101.7
यदyad वाvā मरुत्वःmarutvaḥ परमेparame सधस्थेsadhasthe यदyad वावमेvāvame वर्जनेvṛjane मादयासेmādayāse |

अतata आā याह्यध्वरंyāhyadhvaraṃ नोno अछाachā तवायाtvāyā हविश्चक्र्माhaviścakṛmā सत्यराधःsatyarādhaḥ ||
O girt by Maruts, whether thou delight thee in loftiest gathering-place or lowly dwelling,
Come thence unto our rite, true boon-bestower: through love of thee have we prepared oblations.
1.101.8
तवायेन्द्रtvāyendra सोमंsomaṃ सुषुमाsuṣumā सुदक्षsudakṣa तवायाtvāyā हविश्चक्र्माhaviścakṛmā बरह्मवाहःbrahmavāhaḥ |

अधाadhā नियुत्वःniyutvaḥ सगणोsaghaṇo मरुद्भिरस्मिनmarudbhirasmin यज्ञेyajñe बर्हिषिमादयस्वbarhiṣimādayasva ||
We, fain for thee, strong Indra, have pressed Soma, and, O thou sought with prayer, have made oblations.
Now at this sacrifice, with all thy Maruts, on sacred grass, O team-borne God, rejoice thee.
1.101.9
मादयस्वmādayasva हरिभिर्येharibhirye तta इन्द्रindra विvi षयस्वṣyasva शिप्रेśipre विvi सर्जस्वsṛjasva धेनेdhene |

आā तवाtvā सुशिप्रsuśipra हरयोharayo वहन्तूशनvahantūśan हव्यानिhavyāni परतिprati नोno जुषस्वjuṣasva ||
Rejoice thee with thine own Bay Steeds, O Indra, unclose thy jaws and let thy lips be open.
Thou with the fair cheek, let thy Bay Steeds bring thee: gracious to us, he pleased with our oblation.
1.101.10
मरुत्स्तोत्रस्यmarutstotrasya वर्जनस्यvṛjanasya गोपाghopā वयमिन्द्रेणvayamindreṇa सनुयामsanuyāma वाजमvājam |

तनtan नोno ...... ||
Guards of the camp whose praisers are the Maruts, may we through Indra, get ourselves the booty.
This prayer of ours may Varuṇa grant, and Mitra, and Aditi and Sindhu, Earth and Heaven.
