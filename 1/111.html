HYMN CXI. ṚBHUS.

तक्षनtakṣan रथंrathaṃ सुव्र्तंsuvṛtaṃ विदम्नापसस्तक्षनvidamnāpasastakṣan हरीharī इन्द्रवाहाindravāhā वर्षण्वसूvṛṣaṇvasū |

तक्षनtakṣan पित्र्भ्यांpitṛbhyāṃ रभवोṛbhavo युवदyuvad वयस्तक्षन्वत्सायvayastakṣanvatsāya मातरंmātaraṃ सचाभुवमsacābhuvam ||
WORKING with skill they wrought the lightly rolling car: they wrought the Bays who bear Indra and bring great gifts.
The Ṛbhus for their Parents made life young again; and fashioned for the calf a mother by its side.
1.111.1
आā नोno यज्ञायyajñāya तक्षतtakṣata रभुमदṛbhumad वयःvayaḥ करत्वेkratve दक्षायdakṣāya सुप्रजावतीमिषमsuprajāvatīmiṣam |

यथाyathā कषयामkṣayāma सर्ववीरयाsarvavīrayā विशाviśā तनtan नःशर्धायnaḥśardhāya धासथाdhāsathā सविन्द्रियमsvindriyam ||
For sacrifice make for us active vital power for skill and wisdom food with noble progeny.
Grant to our company this power most excellent, that with a family all-heroic we may dwell.
1.111.2
आā तक्षतtakṣata सातिमस्मभ्यंsātimasmabhyaṃ रभवःṛbhavaḥ सातिंsātiṃ रथायrathāya सातिमर्वतेsātimarvate नरःnaraḥ |

सातिंsātiṃ नोno जैत्रींjaitrīṃ संsaṃ महेतmaheta विश्वहाviśvahā जामिमजामिंjāmimajāmiṃ पर्तनासुpṛtanāsu सक्षणिमsakṣaṇim ||
Do ye, O Ṛbhus, make prosperity for us, prosperity for car, ye Heroes, and for steed.
Grant us prosperity victorious evermore,
conquering foes in battle, strangers or akin.
1.111.3
रभुक्षणमिन्द्रमाṛbhukṣaṇamindramā हुवhuva ऊतयūtaya रभूनṛbhūn वाजानvājān मरुतःmarutaḥ सोमपीतयेsomapītaye |

उभाubhā मित्रावरुणाmitrāvaruṇā नूनमश्विनाnūnamaśvinā तेte नोno हिन्वन्तुhinvantu सातयेsātaye धियेdhiye जिषेjiṣe ||
Indra, the Ṛbhus' Lord, I invocate for aid, the Ṛbhus, Vājas, Maruts to the Soma draught.
Varuṇa, Mitra, both, yea, and the Aśvins Twain: let them speed us to wealth, wisdom, and victory.
1.111.4
रभुर्भरायṛbhurbharāya संsaṃ शिशातुśiśātu सातिंsātiṃ समर्यजिदsamaryajid वाजोvājo अस्मानविष्टुasmānaviṣṭu |

तनtan नोno ...... ||
May Ṛbhu send prosperity for battle, may Vāja conquering in the fight protect us.
This prayer of ours may Varuṇa grant, and Mitra, and Aditi and Sindhu, Earth and Heaven.
