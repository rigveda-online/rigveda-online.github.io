HYMN XCVII. AGNI.

अपapa नःnaḥ शोशुचदघमग्नेśośucadaghamaghne शुशुग्ध्याśuśughdhyā रयिमrayim |

अपapa नःnaḥ शोशुचदघमśośucadagham ||
CHASING with light our sin away, O Agni, shine thou wealth on us.
May his light chase our sin away.
1.97.1
सुक्षेत्रियाsukṣetriyā सुगातुयाsughātuyā वसूयाvasūyā चca यजामहेyajāmahe |

अपapa ...... ||
For goodly fields, for pleasant homes, for wealth we sacrifice to thee.
May his light chase our sin away.
1.97.2
परpra यदyad भन्दिष्ठbhandiṣṭha एषांeṣāṃ परास्माकासश्चprāsmākāsaśca सूरयःsūrayaḥ |

अप...apa... ||
Best praiser of all these be he; foremost, our chiefs who sacrifice.
May his light chase our sin away.
1.97.3
परpra यतyat तेte अग्नेaghne सूरयोsūrayo जायेमहिjāyemahi परpra तेte वयमvayam |

अपapa ...... ||
So that thy worshippers and we, thine, Agni, in our sons may live.
May his light chase our sin away.
1.97.4
परpra यदग्नेःyadaghneḥ सहस्वतोsahasvato विश्वतोviśvato यन्तिyanti भानवःbhānavaḥ |

अपapa ...... ||
As ever-conquering Agni's beams of splendour go to every side,
May his light chase our sin away.
1.97.5
तवंtvaṃ हिhi विश्वतोमुखviśvatomukha विश्वतःviśvataḥ परिभूरसिparibhūrasi |

अपapa ...... ||
To every side thy face is turned, thou art triumphant everywhere.
May his light chase our sin away.
1.97.6
दविषोdviṣo नोno विश्वतोमुखातिviśvatomukhāti नावेवnāveva पारयpāraya |

अपapa ...... ||
O thou whose face looks every way, bear us past foes as in a ship.
May his light chase our sin away.
1.97.7
सsa नःnaḥ सिन्धुमिवsindhumiva नावयातिnāvayāti पर्षाparṣā सवस्तयेsvastaye |

अपapa ...... ||
As in a ship, convey thou us for our advantage o’er the flood.
May his light chase our sin away.
1.97.8
