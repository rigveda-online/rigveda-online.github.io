HYMN CLXXXVI. VIŚVEDEVAS.

आā नna इळभिर्विदथेiḷabhirvidathe सुशस्तिsuśasti विश्वानरःviśvānaraḥ सविताsavitā देवdeva एतुetu |

अपिapi यथाyathā युवानोyuvāno मत्सथाmatsathā नोno विश्वंviśvaṃ जगदभिपित्वेjaghadabhipitve मनीषाmanīṣā ||
LOVED of all men, may Savitar, through praises offered as sacred food, come to our synod,
That you too, through-our hymn, ye ever-youthful, may gladden, at your visit, all our people.
1.186.1
आā नोno विश्वviśva आस्क्राāskrā गमन्तुghamantu देवाdevā मित्रोmitro अर्यमाaryamā वरुणःvaruṇaḥ सजोषाःsajoṣāḥ |

भुवनbhuvan यथाyathā नोno विश्वेviśve वर्धासःvṛdhāsaḥ करनkaran सुषाहाsuṣāhā विथुरंvithuraṃ नna शवःśavaḥ ||
To us may all the Gods come trooped together, Aryaman, Mitra, Varuṇa concordant,
That all may be promoters of our welfare, and with great might preserve our strength from slackness.
1.186.2
परेष्ठंpreṣṭhaṃ वोvo अतिथिंatithiṃ गर्णीषे.अग्निंghṛṇīṣe.aghniṃ शस्तिभिस्तुर्वणिःśastibhisturvaṇiḥ सजोषाःsajoṣāḥ |

असदasad यथाyathā नोno वरुणःvaruṇaḥ सुकीर्तिरिषश्चsukīrtiriṣaśca पर्षदरिगूर्तःparṣadarighūrtaḥ सूरिःsūriḥ ||
Agni I sing, the guest you love most dearly: the Conqueror through our lauds is friendly-minded.
That he may be our Varuṇa rich in glory and send food like a prince praised by the godly.
1.186.3
उपupa वva एषेeṣe नमसाnamasā जिगीषोषासानक्ताjighīṣoṣāsānaktā सुदुघेवsudugheva धेनुःdhenuḥ |

समानेsamāne अहनahan विमिमानोvimimāno अर्कंarkaṃ विषुरूपेviṣurūpe पयसिpayasi सस्मिन्नूधनsasminnūdhan ||
To you I seek with reverence, Night and Morning, like a cow good to milk, with hope to conquer,
Preparing on a common day the praise. song with milk of various hues within this udder.
1.186.4
उतuta नो.अहिर्बुध्न्योno.ahirbudhnyo मयसmayas कःkaḥ शिशुंśiśuṃ नna पिप्युषीवpipyuṣīva वेतिveti सिन्धुःsindhuḥ |

येनyena नपातमपांnapātamapāṃ जुनामjunāma मनोजुवोmanojuvo वर्षणोvṛṣaṇo यंyaṃ वहन्तिvahanti ||
May the great Dragon of the Deep rejoice us: as one who nourishes her young comes Sindhu,
With whom we will incite the Child of Waters whom vigorous course swift as thought bring hither.
1.186.5
उतuta नna ईंīṃ तवष्टाtvaṣṭā गन्त्वछाghantvachā समतsmat सूरिभिरभिपित्वेsūribhirabhipitve सजोषाःsajoṣāḥ |

आā वर्त्रहेन्द्रश्चर्षणिप्रास्तुविष्टमोvṛtrahendraścarṣaṇiprāstuviṣṭamo नरांnarāṃ नैहnaiha गम्याःghamyāḥ ||
Moreover Tvaṣṭar also shall approach us, one-minded with the princes at his visit.
Hither shall come the Vṛtra-slayer Indra, Ruler of men, as strongest of the Heroes.
1.186.6
उतuta नna ईंīṃ मतयो.अश्वयोगःmatayo.aśvayoghaḥ शिशुंśiśuṃ नna गावस्तरुणंghāvastaruṇaṃ रिहन्तिrihanti |

तमींtamīṃ गिरोghiro जनयोjanayo नna पत्नीःpatnīḥ सुरभिष्टमंsurabhiṣṭamaṃ नरांनसन्तnarāṃnasanta ||
Him too our hymns delight, that yoke swift horses, like mother cows who lick their tender youngling.
To him our songs shall yield themselves like spouses, to him the most delightful of the Heroes.
1.186.7
उतuta नna ईंīṃ मरुतोmaruto वर्द्धसेनाःvṛddhasenāḥ समदsmad रोदसीrodasī समनसःsamanasaḥ सदन्तुsadantu |

पर्षदश्वासो.अवनयःpṛṣadaśvāso.avanayaḥ नna रथाrathā रिशादसोriśādaso मित्रयुजोmitrayujo नna देवाःdevāḥ ||
So may the Maruts, armed with mighty weapons, rest here on heaven and earth with hearts in concord,
As Gods whose cars have dappled steeds like torrents, destroyers of the foe allies of Mitra.
1.186.8
परpra नुnu यदेषांyadeṣāṃ महिनाmahinā चिकित्रेcikitre परpra युञ्जतेyuñjate परयुजस्तेprayujaste सुव्र्क्तिsuvṛkti |

अधadha यदेषांyadeṣāṃ सुदिनेsudine नna शरुर्विश्वमेरिणंśarurviśvameriṇaṃ परुषायन्तpruṣāyanta सेनःsenaḥ ||
They hasten on to happy termination their orders when they are made known by
glory.
As on a fair bright day the arrow flieth o’er all the barren soil their missiles sparkle.
1.186.9
परोpro अश्विनाववसेaśvināvavase कर्णुध्वंkṛṇudhvaṃ परpra पूषणंpūṣaṇaṃ सवतवसोsvatavaso हिhi सन्तिsanti |

अद्वेषोadveṣo विष्णुर्वातviṣṇurvāta रभुक्षाṛbhukṣā अछाachā सुम्नायsumnāya वव्र्तीयदेवानvavṛtīyadevān ||
Incline the Aśvins to show grace, and Pūṣan, for power and might have they, their own possession.
Friendly are Viṣṇu, Vāta, and Ṛbhukṣan so may I bring the Gods to make us happy.
1.186.10
इयंiyaṃ साsā वोvo अस्मेasme दीधितिर्यजत्राdīdhitiryajatrā अपिप्राणीapiprāṇī चca सदनीsadanī चca भूयःbhūyaḥ |

निni याyā देवेषुdeveṣu यततेyatate वसूयुर्वि...vasūyurvi... ||
This is my reverent thought of you, ye Holy; may it inspire you, make you dwell among us,—
Thought, toiling for the Gods and seeking treasure. May we find strengthening food in full abundance.
