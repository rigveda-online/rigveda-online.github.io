HYMN C. INDRA.

सsa योyo वर्षाvṛṣā वर्ष्ण्येभिःvṛṣṇyebhiḥ समोकाsamokā महोmaho दिवःdivaḥ पर्थिव्याश्चसम्राटpṛthivyāścasamrāṭ |

सतीनसत्वाsatīnasatvā हव्योhavyo भरेषुbhareṣu मरुत्वानmarutvān नोno भवत्विन्द्रbhavatvindra ऊतीūtī ||
MAY he who hath his home with strength, the Mighty, the King supreme of earth and spacious heaven,
Lord of true power, to he invoked in battles,—may Indra, girt by Maruts, be our succour.
1.100.1
यस्यानाप्तःyasyānāptaḥ सूर्यस्येवsūryasyeva यामोyāmo भरे-भरेbhare-bhare वर्त्रहाvṛtrahā शुष्मोśuṣmo अस्तिasti |

वर्षन्तमःvṛṣantamaḥ सखिभिःsakhibhiḥ सवेभिरेवैर्म...svebhirevairma... ||
Whose way is unattainable like Sūrya's: he in each fight is the strong Vṛtra-slayer,
Mightiest with his Friends in his own courses. May Indra, girt by Maruts, be our succour.
1.100.2
दिवोdivo नna यस्यyasya रेतसोretaso दुघानाःdughānāḥ पन्थासोpanthāso यन्तिyanti शवसापरीताःśavasāparītāḥ |

तरद्द्वेषाःtaraddveṣāḥ सासहिःsāsahiḥ पौंस्येभिर्म...pauṃsyebhirma... ||
Whose paths go forth in their great might resistless, forthmilking, as it were, heaven's genial moisture.
With manly strength triumphant, foe-subduer,—may Indra, girt by Maruts, be our succour.
1.100.3
सोso अङगिरोभिरङगिरस्तमोaṅghirobhiraṅghirastamo भूदbhūd वर्षाvṛṣā वर्षभिःvṛṣabhiḥ सखिभिःsakhibhiḥ सखाsakhā सनsan |

रग्मिभिर्र्ग्मीṛghmibhirṛghmī गातुभिर्ज्येष्ठोghātubhirjyeṣṭho म...ma... ||
Among Aṅgirases he was the chiefest, a Friend with friends, mighty amid the mighty.
Praiser mid praisers, honoured most of singers. May Indra, girt by Maruts, be our succour.
1.100.4
सsa सूनुभिर्नsūnubhirna रुद्रेभिर्र्भ्वाrudrebhirṛbhvā नर्षाह्येnṛṣāhye सासह्वानमित्रानsāsahvānamitrān |

सनीळेभिःsanīḷebhiḥ शरवस्यानिśravasyāni तूर्वनtūrvan म...ma... ||
Strong with the Rudras as with his own children, in manly battle conquering his foemen '
With his close comrades doing deeds of glory,—may Indra, girt by Maruts, be our succour.
1.100.5
सsa मन्युमीःmanyumīḥ समदनस्यsamadanasya कर्तास्माकेभिर्न्र्भिःkartāsmākebhirnṛbhiḥ सूर्यंsūryaṃ सनतsanat |

अस्मिन्नहनasminnahan सत्पतिःsatpatiḥ पुरुहूतोpuruhūto म...ma... ||
Humbler of pride, exciter of the conflict, the Lord of heroes, God invoked of many,
May he this day gain with our men the sunlight. May Indra, girt by Maruts, be our succour.
1.100.6
तमूतयोtamūtayo रणयञ्छूरसातौraṇayañchūrasātau तंtaṃ कषेमस्यkṣemasya कषितयःkṣitayaḥ कर्ण्वतkṛṇvata तरामtrām |

सsa विश्वस्यviśvasya करुणस्येशkaruṇasyeśa एकोeko म...ma... ||
His help hath made him cheerer in the battle, the folk have made him guardian of their comfort.
Sole Lord is he of every holy service. May Indra, girt by Maruts, be our succour.
1.100.7
तमप्सन्तtamapsanta शवसśavasa उत्सवेषुutsaveṣu नरोnaro नरमवसेnaramavase तंtaṃ धनायdhanāya |

सोso अन्धेandhe चितcit तमसिtamasi जयोतिर्विदनjyotirvidan म...ma... ||
To him the Hero, on high days of prowess, heroes for help and booty shall betake them.
He hath found light even in the blinding darkness. May Indra, girt by Maruts, be our succour.
1.100.8
सsa सव्येनsavyena यमतिyamati वराधतश्चितvrādhataścit सsa दक्षिणेdakṣiṇe संग्र्भीताsaṃghṛbhītā कर्तानिkṛtāni |

सsa कीरिणाkīriṇā चितcit सनिताsanitā धनानिdhanāni म...ma... ||
He with his left hand checketh even the mighty, and with his right hand gathereth up the booty.
Even with the humble he acquireth riches. May Indra, girt by Maruts, be our succour.
1.100.9
सsa गरामेभिःghrāmebhiḥ सनिताsanitā सsa रथेभिर्विदेrathebhirvide विश्वाभिःviśvābhiḥ कर्ष्टिभिर्न्वद्यkṛṣṭibhirnvadya |

सsa पौंस्येभिरभिभूरशस्तीर्म...pauṃsyebhirabhibhūraśastīrma... ||
With hosts on foot and cars he winneth treasures: well is he known this day by all the people.
With manly might he conquereth those who hate him. May Indra, girt by Maruts, be our succour.
1.100.10
सsa जामिभिर्यतjāmibhiryat समजातिsamajāti मीळ्हे.अजामिभिर्वाmīḷhe.ajāmibhirvā पुरुहूतpuruhūta एवैःevaiḥ |

अपांapāṃ तोकस्यtokasya तनयस्यtanayasya जेषेjeṣe म...ma... ||
When in his ways with kinsmen or with strangers he speedeth to the fight, invoked of many,
For gain of waters, and of sons and grandsons, may Indra, girt by Maruts, be our succour.
1.100.11
सsa वज्रभ्र्दvajrabhṛd दस्युहाdasyuhā भीमbhīma उग्रःughraḥ सहस्रचेताःsahasracetāḥ शतनीथर्भ्वाśatanīthaṛbhvā |

चम्रीषोcamrīṣo नna शवसाśavasā पाञ्चजन्योpāñcajanyo म...ma... ||
Awful and fierce, fiend-slayer, thunder-wielder, with boundless knowledge, hymned by hundreds, mighty,
In strength like Soma, guard of the Five Peoples, may Indra, girt by Maruts, be our succour.
1.100.12
तस्यtasya वज्रःvajraḥ करन्दतिkrandati समतsmat सवर्षाsvarṣā दिवोdivo नna तवेषोtveṣo रवथःशिमीवानravathaḥśimīvān |

तंtaṃ सचन्तेsacante सनयस्तंsanayastaṃ धनानिdhanāni म...ma... ||
Winning the light, hitherward roars his thunder like the terrific mighty voice of Heaven.
Rich gifts and treasures evermore attend him. May Indra, girt by Maruts, be our succour.
1.100.13
यस्याजस्रंyasyājasraṃ शवसाśavasā मानमुक्थंmānamukthaṃ परिभुजदparibhujad रोदसीrodasī विश्वतःviśvataḥ सीमsīm |

सsa पारिषतpāriṣat करतुभिर्मन्दसानोkratubhirmandasāno म...ma... ||
Whose home eternal through his strength surrounds him on every side, his laud, the earth and heaven,
May he, delighted with our service, save us. May Indra, girt by Maruts, be our succour.
1.100.14
नna यस्यyasya देवाdevā देवताdevatā नna मर्ताmartā आपश्चनāpaścana शवसोśavaso अन्तमापुःantamāpuḥ |

सsa पररिक्वाprarikvā तवक्षसाtvakṣasā कष्मोkṣmo दिवश्चdivaśca म...ma... ||
The limit of whose power not Gods by Godhead, nor mortal men have reached, nor yet the Waters.
Both Earth and Heaven in vigour he surpasseth. May Indra, girt by Maruts, he our succour.
1.100.15
रोहिच्छ्यावाrohicchyāvā सुमदंशुर्ललामीर्द्युक्षाsumadaṃśurlalāmīrdyukṣā रायrāya रज्राश्वस्यṛjrāśvasya |

वर्षण्वन्तंvṛṣaṇvantaṃ बिभ्रतीbibhratī धूर्षुdhūrṣu रथंrathaṃ मन्द्राmandrā चिकेतciketa नाहुषीषुnāhuṣīṣu विक्षुvikṣu ||
The red and tawny mare, blaze-marked, high standing, celestial who, to bring Ṛjrāśva riches,
Drew at the pole the chariot yoked with stallions, joyous, among the hosts of men was noted.
1.100.16
एततetat तयतtyat तta इन्द्रindra वर्ष्णvṛṣṇa उक्थंukthaṃ वार्षागिराvārṣāghirā अभिabhi गर्णन्तिghṛṇanti राधःrādhaḥ |

रज्राश्वःṛjrāśvaḥ परष्टिभिरम्बरीषःpraṣṭibhirambarīṣaḥ सहदेवोsahadevo भयमानःbhayamānaḥ सुराधाःsurādhāḥ ||
The Vārṣāgiras unto thee, O Indra, the Mighty One, sing forth this laud to please thee,
Ṛjrāśva with his fellows, Ambarīṣa, Surādhas, Sahadeva, Bhayamāna.
1.100.17
दस्यूञ्छिम्यूंश्चdasyūñchimyūṃśca पुरुहूतpuruhūta एवैर्हत्वाevairhatvā पर्थिव्यांpṛthivyāṃ शर्वाśarvā निni बर्हीतbarhīt |

सनतsanat कषेत्रंkṣetraṃ सखिभिःsakhibhiḥ शवित्न्येभिःśvitnyebhiḥ सनत्सूर्यंsanatsūryaṃ सनदपःsanadapaḥ सुवज्रःsuvajraḥ ||
He, much invoked, hath slain Dasyus and Śimyus, after his wont, and laid them low with arrows.
The mighty Thunderer with his fair-complexioned friends won the land, the sunlight, and the waters.
1.100.18
विश्वाहेन्द्रोviśvāhendro अधिवक्ताadhivaktā नोno अस्त्वपरिह्व्र्ताःastvaparihvṛtāḥ सनुयामsanuyāma वाजमvājam |

तनtan नोno ...... ||
May Indra evermore be our protector, and unimperilled may we win the booty.
This prayer of ours may Varuṇa grant, and Mitra, and Aditi and Sindhu, Earth and Heaven.
