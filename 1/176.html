HYMN CLXXVI. INDRA.

मत्सिmatsi नोno वस्यैष्टयvasyaiṣṭaya इन्द्रमिन्दोindramindo वर्षाvṛṣā विशviśa |

रघायमाणैन्वसिṛghāyamāṇainvasi शत्रुमन्तिśatrumanti नna विन्दसिvindasi ||
CHEER thee with draughts to win us bliss: Soma, pierce Indra in thy strength.
Thou stormest trembling in thy rage, and findest not a foeman nigh.
1.176.1
तस्मिन्नाtasminnā वेशयाveśayā गिरोghiro यya एकश्चर्षणीनामekaścarṣaṇīnām |

अनुanu सवधायमुप्यतेsvadhāyamupyate यवंyavaṃ नna चर्क्र्षदcarkṛṣad वर्षाvṛṣā ||
Make our songs penetrate to him who is the Only One of men;
For whom the sacred food is spread, as the steer ploughs the barley in.
1.176.2
यस्यyasya विश्वानिviśvāni हस्तयोःhastayoḥ पञ्चpañca कषितीनांkṣitīnāṃ वसुvasu |

सपाशयस्वspāśayasva योyo अस्मध्रुगasmadhrugh दिव्येवाशनिर्जहिdivyevāśanirjahi ||
Within whose hands deposited all the Five Peoples’ treasures rest.
Mark thou the man who injures us and kill him like the heavenly bolt.
1.176.3
असुन्वन्तंasunvantaṃ समंsamaṃ जहिjahi दूणाशंdūṇāśaṃ योyo नna तेte मयःmayaḥ |

अस्मभ्यमस्यasmabhyamasya वेदनंvedanaṃ दद्धिdaddhi सूरिश्चिदोहतेsūriścidohate ||
Slay everyone who pours no gift, who, hard to reach, delights thee not.
Bestow on us what wealth he hath: this even the worshipper awaits.
1.176.4
आवोāvo यस्यyasya दविबर्हसो.अर्केषुdvibarhaso.arkeṣu सानुषगसतsānuṣaghasat |

आजाविन्द्रस्येन्दोājāvindrasyendo परावोprāvo वाजेषुvājeṣu वाजिनमvājinam ||
Thou helpest him the doubly strong whose hymns were sung unceasingly.
When Indra fought, O Soma, thou helpest the mighty in the fray.
1.176.5
यथाyathā पूर्वेभ्योpūrvebhyo ...... ||
As thou, O Indra, to the ancient singers wast ever joy, like water to the thirsty,
So unto thee I sing this invocation. May we find strengthening food in full abundance.
1.176.6
