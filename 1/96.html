HYMN XCVI. AGNI.

सsa परत्नथाpratnathā सहसाsahasā जायमानःjāyamānaḥ सद्यःsadyaḥ काव्यानिkāvyāni बळbaḷ अधत्तadhatta विश्वाviśvā |

अपश्चapaśca मित्रंmitraṃ धिषणाdhiṣaṇā चca साधनsādhan देवाdevā अग्निन्धारयनaghnindhārayan दरविणोदामdraviṇodām ||
HE in the ancient way by strength engendered, lo! straight hath taken to himself all wisdom.
The waters and the bowl have made him friendly. The Gods possessed the wealth bestowing Agni.
1.96.1
सsa पूर्वयाpūrvayā निविदाnividā कव्यतायोरिमाःkavyatāyorimāḥ परजाprajā अजनयनajanayan मनूनामmanūnām |

विवस्वताvivasvatā चक्षसाcakṣasā दयामपश्चdyāmapaśca देवाdevā अ.a. ध.dh. द.d. ||
At Āyu's ancient call he by his wisdom gave all this progeny of men their being,
And, by refulgent light, heaven and the waters. The Gods possessed the wealth. bestowing Agni.
1.96.2
तमीळतtamīḷata परथमंprathamaṃ यज्ञसाधंyajñasādhaṃ विशviśa आरीराहुतंārīrāhutaṃ रञ्जसानमṛñjasānam |

ऊर्जःūrjaḥ पुत्रंputraṃ भरतंbharataṃ सर्प्रदानुंsṛpradānuṃ देवाdevā ...... ||
Praise him, ye Āryan folk, as chief performer of sacrifice adored and ever toiling,
Well-tended, Son of Strength, the Constant Giver. The Gods possessed the wealth bestowing Agni.
1.96.3
सsa मातरिश्वाmātariśvā पुरुवारपुष्टिर्विददpuruvārapuṣṭirvidad गातुंghātuṃ तनयायtanayāya सवर्वितsvarvit |

विशांviśāṃ गोपाghopā जनिताjanitā रोदस्योर्देवाrodasyordevā ...... ||
That Mātariśvan rich in wealth and treasure, light-winner, finds a pathway for his offspring.
Guard of our folk, Father of earth and heaven. The Gods possessed the wealth bestowing Agni.
1.96.4
नक्तोषासाnaktoṣāsā वर्णमामेम्यानेvarṇamāmemyāne धापयेतेdhāpayete शिशुमेकंśiśumekaṃ समीचीsamīcī |

दयावाक्षामाdyāvākṣāmā रुक्मोrukmo अन्तर्विantarvi भातिbhāti देवाdevā ...... ||
Night and Dawn, changing each the other's colour, meeting together suckle one same Infant:
Golden between the heaven and earth he shineth. The Gods possessed the wealth bestowing Agni.
1.96.5
रायोrāyo बुध्नःbudhnaḥ संगमनोsaṃghamano वसूनांvasūnāṃ यज्ञस्यyajñasya केतुर्मन्मसाधनोketurmanmasādhano वेःveḥ |

अम्र्तत्वंamṛtatvaṃ रक्षमाणासrakṣamāṇāsa एनंenaṃ देवाdevā ...... ||
Root of wealth, gathering-place of treasures, banner of sacrifice, who grants the suppliant's wishes:
Preserving him as their own life immortal, the Gods possessed the wealth-bestowing Agni.
1.96.6
नूnū चca पुराpurā चca सदनंsadanaṃ रयीणांrayīṇāṃ जातस्यjātasya चca जायमानस्यjāyamānasya चca कषामkṣām |

सतश्चsataśca गोपांghopāṃ भवतश्चbhavataśca भूरेर्देवाbhūrerdevā ...... ||
Now and of old the home of wealth, the mansion of what is born and what was born aforetime,
Guard of what is and what will be hereafter,—the Gods possessed the wealth bestowing Agni.
1.96.7
दरविणोदाdraviṇodā दरविणसस्तुरस्यdraviṇasasturasya दरविणोदाःdraviṇodāḥ सनरस्यsanarasya परpra यंसतyaṃsat |

दरविणोदाdraviṇodā वीरवतीमिषंvīravatīmiṣaṃ नोno दरविणोदाdraviṇodā रसतेrasate दीर्घमायुःdīrghamāyuḥ ||
May the Wealth-Giver grant us conquering riches; may the Wealth-Giver grant us wealth with heroes.
May the Wealth-Giver grant us food with offspring, and length of days may the Wealth-Giver send us.
1.96.8
एवाevā नोno अग्नेaghne समिधाsamidhā वर्धानोvṛdhāno रेवतrevat पावकpāvaka शरवसेśravase विvi भाहिbhāhi ||
Fed with our fuel, purifying Agni, so blaze to us auspiciously for glory.
This prayer of ours may Varuṇa grant, and Mitra, and Aditi and Sindhu, Earth and Heaven.
1.96.9
तनtan नोno मित्रोmitro वरुणोvaruṇo मामहन्तामmāmahantām अदितिःaditiḥ सिन्धुःsindhuḥ पर्थ्विवीpṛthvivī उतोuto दयौःdyauḥ ||
