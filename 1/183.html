HYMN CLXXXIII. AŚVINS.

तंtaṃ युञ्जाथांyuñjāthāṃ मनसोmanaso योyo जवीयानjavīyān तरिवन्धुरोtrivandhuro वर्षणvṛṣaṇa यस्त्रिचक्रःyastricakraḥ |

येनोपयाथःyenopayāthaḥ सुक्र्तोsukṛto दुरोणंduroṇaṃ तरिधातुनtridhātuna पतथोविर्नpatathovirna पर्णैःparṇaiḥ ||
MAKE ready that which passes thought in swiftness, that hath three wheels and triple seat, ye Mighty,
Whereon ye seek the dwelling of the pious, whereon, threefold, ye fly like birds with pinions.
1.183.1
सुव्र्दsuvṛd रथोratho वर्ततेvartate यन्नभिyannabhi कषांkṣāṃ यतyat तिष्ठथःtiṣṭhathaḥ करतुमन्तानुkratumantānu पर्क्षेpṛkṣe |

वपुर्वपुष्याvapurvapuṣyā सचतामियंsacatāmiyaṃ गीर्दिवोghīrdivo दुहित्रोषसाduhitroṣasā सचेथेsacethe ||
Light rolls your easy chariot faring earthward, what time, for food, ye, full of wisdom, mount it.
May this song, wondrous fair, attend your glory: ye, as ye travel, wait on Dawn Heaven's Daughter.
1.183.2
आā तिष्ठतंtiṣṭhataṃ सुव्र्तंsuvṛtaṃ योyo रथोratho वामनुvāmanu वरतानिvratāni वर्ततेvartate हविष्मानhaviṣmān |

येनyena नराnarā नासत्येषयध्यैnāsatyeṣayadhyai वर्तिर्याथस्तनयायत्मनेvartiryāthastanayāyatmane चca ||
Ascend your lightly rolling car, approaching the worshipper who turns him to his duties,—
Whereon ye come unto the house to quicken man and his offspring, O Nāsatyas, Heroes.
1.183.3
माmā वांvāṃ वर्कोvṛko माmā वर्कीराvṛkīrā दधर्षीनdadharṣīn माmā परिpari वर्क्तमुतमातिvarktamutamāti धक्तमdhaktam |

अयंayaṃ वांvāṃ भागोbhāgho निहितnihita इयंiyaṃ गीर्दस्राविमेghīrdasrāvime वांvāṃ निधयोnidhayo मधूनामmadhūnām ||
Let not the wolf, let not the she-wolf harm you. Forsake me not, nor pass me by or others.
Here stands your share, here is your hymn, ye Mighty: yours are these vessels, full of pleasant juices.
1.183.4
युवांyuvāṃ गोतमःghotamaḥ पुरुमीळ्होpurumīḷho अत्रिर्दस्राatrirdasrā हवते.अवसेhavate.avase हविष्मानhaviṣmān |

दिशंdiśaṃ नna दिष्टांdiṣṭāṃ रजूयेवṛjūyeva यन्ताyantā मेme हवंhavaṃ नासत्योपnāsatyopa यातमyātam ||
Gotama, Purumīlha, Atri bringing oblations all invoke you for protection.
Like one who goes straight to the point directed, ye Nāsatyas, to mine invocation.
1.183.5
अतारिष्मatāriṣma तमससtamasas पारमस्यpāramasya परतिprati वांvāṃ सतोमोstomo अश्विनावधायिaśvināvadhāyi |

एहeha यातंyātaṃ पथिभिर्देवयानैर्वि...pathibhirdevayānairvi... ||
We have passed o’er the limit of this darkness: our praise hath been bestowed on you, O Aśvins.
Come hitherward by paths which Gods have travelled. May we find strengthening food in full abundance.
1.183.6
