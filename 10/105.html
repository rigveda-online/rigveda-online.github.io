HYMN CV. INDRA.

कदाkadā वसोvaso सतोत्रंstotraṃ हर्यतharyata आवāva शमशाśmaśā रुधदrudhad वाःvāḥ |

दीर्घंdīrghaṃ सुतंsutaṃ वाताप्यायvātāpyāya ||
WHEN, Vasu, wilt thou love the laud? Now let the channel bring the stream.
The juice is ready to ferment.
10.105.1
हरीharī यस्यyasya सुयुजाsuyujā विव्रताvivratā वेरर्वन्तानुverarvantānu शेपाśepā |

उभारजीubhārajī नna केशिनाkeśinā पतिर्दनpatirdan ||
He whose two Bay Steeds harnessed well, swerving, pursue the Bird's tail-plumes,
With Rowing manes, like heaven and earth, he is the Lord with power to give.
10.105.2
अपapa योरिन्द्रःyorindraḥ पापजpāpaja आā मर्तोmarto नna शश्रमाणोśaśramāṇo बिभीवानbibhīvān |

शुभेśubhe यदyad युयुजेyuyuje तविषीवानtaviṣīvān ||
Bereft of skill is Indra, if, like some outwearied man he fears
The sinner, when the Mighty hath prepared himself for victory.
10.105.3
सचायोरिन्द्रश्चर्क्र्षsacāyorindraścarkṛṣa आनुपानसःānupānasaḥ सपर्यनsaparyan |

नदयोर्विव्रतयोःnadayorvivratayoḥ शूरśūra इन्द्रःindraḥ ||
Indra with these drives round, until he meets with one to worship him:
Indra is Master of the pair who snort and swerve upon their way.
10.105.4
अधिadhi यस्तस्थौyastasthau केशवन्ताkeśavantā वयचस्वन्ताvyacasvantā नna पुष्ट्यैpuṣṭyai |

वनोतिvanoti शिप्राभ्यांśiprābhyāṃ शिप्रिणीवानśipriṇīvān ||
Borne onward by the long-maned Steeds who stretch themselves as ’twere for food,
The God who wears the helm defends them with his jaws.
10.105.5
परास्तौदprāstaud रष्वौजाṛṣvaujā रष्वेभिस्ततक्षṛṣvebhistatakṣa शूरःśūraḥ शवसाśavasā |

रभुर्नṛbhurna करतुभिर्मातरिश्वाkratubhirmātariśvā ||
The Mighty sang with Lofty Ones: the Hero fashioned with his strength,
Like skilful Mātariśvan with his power and might,
10.105.6
वज्रंvajraṃ यश्चक्रेyaścakre सुहनायsuhanāya दस्यवेdasyave हिरीमशोhirīmaśo हिरीमानhirīmān |

अरुतहनुरद्भुतंarutahanuradbhutaṃ नna रजःrajaḥ ||
The bolt, which pierced at once the vitals of the Dasyu easy to be slain,
With jaw uninjured like the wondrous firmament.
10.105.7
अवava नोno वर्जिनाvṛjinā शिशीह्यśiśīhy रचाṛcā वनेमान्र्चःvanemānṛcaḥ |

नाब्रह्माnābrahmā यज्ञर्धगyajñaṛdhagh जोषतिjoṣati तवेtve ||
Grind off our sins: with song will we conquer the men who sing no hymns:
Not easily art thou pleased with prayerless sacrifice.
10.105.8
ऊर्ध्वाūrdhvā यतyat तेte तरेतिनीtretinī भूदbhūd यज्ञस्यyajñasya धूर्षुdhūrṣu सद्मनsadman |

सजूर्नावंsajūrnāvaṃ सवयशसंsvayaśasaṃ सचायोःsacāyoḥ ||
When threefold flame burns high for thee, to rest on poles of sacrifice,
Thou with the living joyest in the self-bright Ship.
10.105.9
शरियेśriye तेte पर्श्निरुपसेचनीpṛśnirupasecanī भूच्छ्रियेbhūcchriye दर्विररेपाःdarvirarepāḥ |

ययाyayā सवेsve पात्रेpātre सिञ्चसsiñcasa उतut ||
Thy glory was the speckled cup, thy glory was the flawless scoop.
Wherewith thou pourest into thy receptacle.
10.105.10
शतंśataṃ वाvā यदसुर्यyadasurya परतिprati तवाtvā सुमित्रsumitra इत्थास्तौदitthāstaud दुर्मित्रैत्थास्तौdurmitraitthāstau |

आवोāvo यदyad दस्युहत्येdasyuhatye कुत्सपुत्रंkutsaputraṃ परावोprāvo यद्दस्युहत्येyaddasyuhatye कुत्सवत्समkutsavatsam ||
As hundreds, O Immortal God, have sung to thee, so hath Sumitra, yea, Durmitra praised thee here,
What time thou holpest Kutsa's son, when Dasyus fell, yea, holpest Kutsa's darling when the Dasyus died.
10.105.11
