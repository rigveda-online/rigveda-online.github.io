HYMN CXV1 INDRA.

पिबाpibā सोमंsomaṃ महतmahata इन्द्रियायindriyāya पिबाpibā वर्त्रायvṛtrāya हन्तवेशविष्ठhantaveśaviṣṭha |

पिबpiba रायेrāye शवसेśavase हूयमानःhūyamānaḥ पिबpiba मध्वस्त्र्पदिन्द्राmadhvastṛpadindrā वर्षस्वvṛṣasva ||
DRINK Soma juice for mighty power and vigour, drink, Strongest One, that thou mayst smite down Vṛtra.
Drink thou, invoked, for strength, and riches: drink thou thy fill of meath and pour it down, O Indra.
10.116.1
अस्यasya पिबpiba कषुमतःkṣumataḥ परस्थितस्येन्द्रprasthitasyendra सोमस्यsomasya वरमासुतश्यvaramāsutaśya |

सवस्तिदाsvastidā मनसाmanasā मादयस्वार्वाचीनोmādayasvārvācīno रेवतेसौभगायrevatesaubhaghāya ||
Drink of the foodful juice stirred into motion, drink what thou choosest of the flowing Soma.
Giver of weal, be joyful in thy spirit, and turn thee hitherward to bless and prosper.
10.116.2
ममत्तुmamattu तवाtvā दिव्यःdivyaḥ सोमsoma इन्द्रindra ममत्तुmamattu यःyaḥ सूयतेपार्थिवेषुsūyatepārthiveṣu |

ममत्तुmamattu येनyena वरिवश्चकर्थvarivaścakartha ममत्तुmamattu येननिरिणासिyenaniriṇāsi शत्रूनśatrūn ||
Let heavenly Soma gladden thee, O Indra, let that effused among mankind delight thee.
Rejoice in that whereby thou gavest freedom, and that whereby thou conquerest thy foemen.
10.116.3
आā दविबर्हाdvibarhā अमिनोamino यात्विन्द्रोyātvindro वर्षाvṛṣā हरिभ्यांharibhyāṃ परिषिक्तमन्धःpariṣiktamandhaḥ |

गव्याghavyā सुतस्यsutasya परभ्र्तस्यprabhṛtasya मध्वःmadhvaḥ सत्राsatrā खेदामरुशहाkhedāmaruśahā वर्षस्वvṛṣasva ||
Let Indra come, impetuous, doubly mighty, to the poured juice, the Bull, with two Bay Coursers.
With juices pressed in milk, with meath presented, glut evermore thy bolt, O Foe-destroyer.
10.116.4
निni तिग्मानिtighmāni भराशयनbhrāśayan भराश्यान्यवbhrāśyānyava सथिराsthirā तनुहियातुजूनामtanuhiyātujūnām |

उग्रायughrāya तेte सहोsaho बलंbalaṃ ददामिdadāmi परतीत्याशत्रूनpratītyāśatrūn विगदेषुvighadeṣu वर्श्चvṛśca ||
Dash down, outffaming their sharp flaming weapons, the strong-holds of the men urged on by demons.
I give thee, Mighty One, great strength and conquest: go, meet thy foes and rend them in the battle.
10.116.5
वयर्यvyarya इन्द्रindra तनुहिtanuhi शरवांस्योजśravāṃsyoja सथिरेवsthireva धन्वनोऽभिमातीःdhanvano'bhimātīḥ |

अस्मद्र्यगasmadryagh वाव्र्धानःvāvṛdhānaḥ सहोभिरनिभ्र्ष्टस्तन्वंsahobhiranibhṛṣṭastanvaṃ वाव्र्धस्वvāvṛdhasva ||
Extend afar the votary's fame and glory, as the firm archer's strength drives off the foeman.
Ranged on our side, grown strong in might that conquers, never defeated, still increase thy body.
10.116.6
इदंidaṃ हविर्मघवनhavirmaghavan तुभ्यंtubhyaṃ रातंrātaṃ परतिprati सम्राळsamrāḷ अह्र्णानोग्र्भायahṛṇānoghṛbhāya |

तुभ्यंtubhyaṃ सुतोsuto मघवनmaghavan तुभ्यंtubhyaṃ पक्वो.अद्धीन्द्रpakvo.addhīndra पिबचpibaca परस्थितस्यprasthitasya ||
To thee have we presented this oblation: accept it, Sovran Ruler, free from anger.
Juice, Maghavan, for thee is pressed and ripened: eat, Indra, drink of that which stirs to meet thee.
10.116.7
अद्धीदिन्द्रaddhīdindra परस्थितेमाprasthitemā हवींषिhavīṃṣi चनोcano दधिष्वdadhiṣva पचतोतसोममpacatotasomam |

परयस्वन्तःprayasvantaḥ परतिprati हर्यामसिharyāmasi तवाtvā सत्याःsatyāḥ सन्तुयजमानस्यsantuyajamānasya कामाःkāmāḥ ||
Eat, Indra, these oblations which approach thee: be pleased with food made ready and with Soma.
With entertainment we receive thee friendly: effectual be the sacrificer's wishes.
10.116.8
परेन्द्राग्निभ्यांprendrāghnibhyāṃ सुवचस्यामियर्मिsuvacasyāmiyarmi सिन्धाविवsindhāviva परेरयंनावमर्कैःprerayaṃnāvamarkaiḥ |

अयाayā इवiva परिpari चरन्तिcaranti देवाdevā येye अस्मभ्यन्धनदाasmabhyandhanadā उद्भिदश्चudbhidaśca ||
I send sweet speech to Indra and to Agni: with hymns I speed it like a boat through waters.
Even thus, the Gods seem moving round about me, the fountains and bestowers of our riches.
10.116.9
