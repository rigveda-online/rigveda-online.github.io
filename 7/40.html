HYMN XL. VIŚVEDEVAS.

ओo शरुष्टिर्विदथ्याśruṣṭirvidathyā समेतुsametu परतिprati सतोमंstomaṃ दधीमहिdadhīmahi तुराणामturāṇām |

यदद्यyadadya देवःdevaḥ सविताsavitā सुवातिsuvāti सयामास्यsyāmāsya रत्निनोratnino विभागेvibhāghe ||
BE gathered all the audience of the synod: let us begin their praise whose course is rapid.
Whate’er God Savitar this day produces, may we be where the Wealthy One distributes.
7.40.1
मित्रस्तनmitrastan नोno वरुणोvaruṇo रोदसीrodasī चca दयुभक्तमिन्द्रोdyubhaktamindro अर्यमाaryamā ददातुdadātu |

दिदेष्टुdideṣṭu देव्यदितीdevyaditī रेक्णोrekṇo वायुश्चvāyuśca यनyan नियुवैतेniyuvaite भगश्चbhaghaśca ||
This, dealt from heaven ' may both the Worlds vouchsafe us, and Varuṇa, Indra, Aryaman, and Mitra.
May Goddess Aditi assign us riches, Vāyu and Bhaga make them ours for ever.
7.40.2
सेदुग्रोsedughro अस्तुastu मरुतःmarutaḥ सsa शुष्मीśuṣmī यंyaṃ मर्त्यंmartyaṃ पर्षदश्वाpṛṣadaśvā अवाथavātha |

उतेमग्निःutemaghniḥ सरस्वतीsarasvatī जुनन्तिjunanti नna तस्यtasya रायःrāyaḥ पर्येतास्तिparyetāsti ||
Strong be the man and full of power, O Maruts, whom ye, borne on by spotted coursers, favour.
Him, too, Sarasvatī and Agni further, and there is none to rob him of his riches.
7.40.3
अयंayaṃ हिhi नेताnetā वरुणvaruṇa रतस्यṛtasya मित्रोmitro राजानोrājāno अर्यमापोaryamāpo धुःdhuḥ |

सुहवाsuhavā देव्यदितिरनर्वाdevyaditiranarvā तेte नोno अंहोaṃho अतिati पर्षन्नरिष्टानparṣannariṣṭān ||
This Varuṇa is guide of Law, he, Mitra, and Aryaman, the Kings, our work have finished.
Divine and foeless Aditi quickly listens. May these deliver us unharmed from trouble.
7.40.4
अस्यasya देवस्यdevasya मीळ्हुषोmīḷhuṣo वयाvayā विष्णोरेषस्यviṣṇoreṣasya परभ्र्थेprabhṛthe हविर्भिःhavirbhiḥ |

विदेvide हिhi रुद्रोrudro रुद्रियंrudriyaṃ महित्वंmahitvaṃ यासिष्टंyāsiṣṭaṃ वर्तिरश्विनाविरावतvartiraśvināvirāvat ||
With offerings I propitiate the branches of this swift-moving God, the bounteous Viṣṇu.
Hence Rudra gained his Rudra-strength: O Aśvins, ye sought the house that hath celestial viands.
7.40.5
मात्रmātra पूषन्नाघ्र्णpūṣannāghṛṇa इरस्योirasyo वरूत्रीvarūtrī यदyad रातिषाचश्चrātiṣācaśca रासनrāsan |

मयोभुवोmayobhuvo नोno अर्वन्तोarvanto निni पान्तुpāntu वर्ष्टिंvṛṣṭiṃ परिज्माparijmā वातोvāto ददातुdadātu ||
Be not thou angry here, O glowing Pūṣan, for what Varūtrī and the Bounteous gave us.
May the swift-moving Gods protect and bless us, and Vāta send us rain, wha wanders round us.
7.40.6
नूnū रोदसीrodasī ...... ||
Now have both worlds been praised by the Vasisthas, and holy Mitra, Varuṇa, and Agni.
May they, bright Deities, make our song supremest. Preserve us evermore, ye Gods, with blessings.
