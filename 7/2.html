HYMN II. ĀPRĪS.

जुषस्वjuṣasva नःnaḥ समिधमग्नेsamidhamaghne अद्यadya शोचाśocā बर्हदbṛhad यजतंyajataṃ धूमम्र्ण्वनdhūmamṛṇvan |

उपupa सप्र्शspṛśa दिव्यंdivyaṃ सानुsānu सतूपैःstūpaiḥ संsaṃ रश्मिभिस्ततनःraśmibhistatanaḥ सूर्यस्यsūryasya ||
GLADLY accept, this day, our fuel, Agni: send up thy sacred smoke and shine sublimely.
Touch the celestial summits with thy columns, and overspread thee with the rays of Sūrya.
7.2.1
नराशंसस्यnarāśaṃsasya महिमानमेषामुपmahimānameṣāmupa सतोषामstoṣāma यजतस्यyajatasya यज्ञैःyajñaiḥ |

येye सुक्रतवःsukratavaḥ शुचयोśucayo धियन्धाःdhiyandhāḥ सवदन्तिsvadanti देवाdevā उभयानिubhayāni हव्याhavyā ||
With sacrifice to these we men will honour the majesty of holy Narāśaṁsa-
To these the pure, most wise, the thought. inspirers, Gods who enjoy both sorts of our oblations.
7.2.2
ईळेन्यंīḷenyaṃ वोvo असुरंasuraṃ सुदक्षमन्तर्दूतंsudakṣamantardūtaṃ रोदसीrodasī सत्यवाचमsatyavācam |

मनुष्वदग्निंmanuṣvadaghniṃ मनुनाmanunā समिद्धंsamiddhaṃ समध्वरायsamadhvarāya सदमिनsadamin महेमmahema ||
We will extol at sacrifice for ever, as men may do, Agni whom Manu kindled,
Your very skilful Asura, meet for worship, envoy between both worlds, the truthful speaker.
7.2.3
सपर्यवोsaparyavo भरमाणाbharamāṇā अभिज्ञुabhijñu परpra वर्ञ्जतेvṛñjate नमसाnamasā बर्हिरग्नौbarhiraghnau |

आजुह्वानाājuhvānā घर्तप्र्ष्ठंghṛtapṛṣṭhaṃ पर्षद्वदध्वर्यवोpṛṣadvadadhvaryavo हविषाhaviṣā मर्जयध्वमmarjayadhvam ||
Bearing the sacred grass, the men who serve him strew it with reverence, on their knees, by Agni.
Calling him to the spotted grass, oil-sprinkled, adorn him, ye Adhvaryus, with oblation.
7.2.4
सवाध्योsvādhyo विvi दुरोduro देवयन्तो.अशिश्रयूdevayanto.aśiśrayū रथयुर्देवताताrathayurdevatātā |

पूर्वीpūrvī शिशुंśiśuṃ नna मातराmātarā रिहाणेrihāṇe समग्रुवोsamaghruvo नna समनेष्वञ्जनsamaneṣvañjan ||
With holy thoughts the pious have thrown open Doors fain for chariots in the Gods’ assembly.
Like two full mother cows who lick their youngling, like maidens for the gathering, they adorn them.
7.2.5
उतuta योषणेyoṣaṇe दिव्येdivye महीmahī नna उषासानक्ताuṣāsānaktā सुदुघेवsudugheva धेनुःdhenuḥ |

बर्हिषदाbarhiṣadā पुरुहूतेpuruhūte मघोनीmaghonī आā यज्ञियेyajñiye सुवितायsuvitāya शरयेतामśrayetām ||
And let the two exalted Heavenly Ladies, Morning and Night, like a cow good at milking,
Come, much-invoked, and on our grass be seated ' wealthy, deserving worship, for our welfare.
7.2.6
विप्राviprā यज्ञेषुyajñeṣu मानुषेषुmānuṣeṣu कारूkārū मन्येmanye वांvāṃ जातवेदसाjātavedasā यजध्यैyajadhyai |

ऊर्ध्वंūrdhvaṃ नोno अध्वरंadhvaraṃ कर्तंkṛtaṃ हवेषुhaveṣu ताtā देवेषुdeveṣu वनथोvanatho वार्याणिvāryāṇi ||
You, Bards and Singers at men's sacrifices, both filled with wisdom, I incline to worship.
Send up our offerings when we call upon you, and so among the Gods obtain us treasures.
7.2.7
आā भारतीbhāratī भारतीभिःbhāratībhiḥ ...... ||
May Bhāratī with all her Sisters, Iḷā accordant with the Gods, with mortals Agni,
Sarasvatī with all her kindred Rivers, come to this grass, Three Goddesses, and seat them.
7.2.8
तनtan नस्तुरीपंnasturīpaṃ ...... ||
Well pleased with us do thou, O God, O Tvaṣṭar, give ready issue to our procreant vigour,
Whence springs the hero, powerful, skilled in action, lover of Gods, adjuster of the press-stones.
7.2.9
वनस्पते.अवvanaspate.ava ...... ||
Send to the Gods the oblation, Lord of Forests, and let the Immolator, Agni, dress it.
He as the truer Priest shall offer worship, for the God'sgenerations well he knoweth.
7.2.10
आā याह्यग्नेyāhyaghne ...... ||
Come thou to us, O Agni, duly kindled, together with the potent Gods and Indra.
On this our grass sit Aditi, happy Mother, and let our Hail! delight the Gods Immortal.
