HYMN XXIX INDRA.

अयंayaṃ सोमsoma इन्द्रindra तुभ्यंtubhyaṃ सुन्वsunva आā तुtu परpra याहिyāhi हरिवस्तदोकाःharivastadokāḥ |

पिबाpibā तवस्यtvasya सुषुतस्यsuṣutasya चारोर्ददोcārordado मघानिmaghāni मघवन्नियानःmaghavanniyānaḥ ||
THIS Soma hath been pressed for thee, O Indra: come hither, Lord of Bays, for this thou lovest.
Drink of this fair, this well-effused libation: Maghavan, give us wealth when we implore thee.
7.29.1
बरह्मनbrahman वीरvīra बरह्मक्र्तिंbrahmakṛtiṃ जुषाणो.अर्वाचीनोjuṣāṇo.arvācīno हरिभिर्याहिharibhiryāhi तूयमtūyam |

अस्मिन्नूasminnū षुṣu सवनेsavane मादयस्वोपmādayasvopa बरह्माणिbrahmāṇi शर्णवśṛṇava इमाimā नःnaḥ ||
Come to us quickly with thy Bay Steeds, Hero, come to our prayer, accepting our devotion.
Enjoy thyself aright at this libation, and listen thou unto the prayers we offer.
7.29.2
काkā तेte अस्त्यरंक्र्तिःastyaraṃkṛtiḥ सूक्तैःsūktaiḥ कदाkadā नूनंnūnaṃ तेte मघवनmaghavan दाशेमdāśema |

विश्वाviśvā मतीराmatīrā ततनेtatane तवायाधाtvāyādhā मma इन्द्रindra शर्णवोśṛṇavo हवेमाhavemā ||
What satisfaction do our hymns afford thee? When, Maghavan? Now let us do thee service.
Hymns, only hymns, with love for thee, I weave thee: then hear, O Indra, these mine invocations.
7.29.3
उतोuto घाghā तेte पुरुष्याpuruṣyā इदासनidāsan येषांyeṣāṃ पूर्वेषामश्र्णोरषीणामpūrveṣāmaśṛṇorṣīṇām |

अधाहंadhāhaṃ तवाtvā मघवञmaghavañ जोहवीमिjohavīmi तवंtvaṃ नna इन्द्रासिindrāsi परमतिःpramatiḥ पितेवpiteva ||
They, verily, were also human beings whom thou wast wont to hear, those earlier sages.
Hence I, O Indra Maghavan, invoke thee: thou art our Providence, even as a Father.
7.29.4
वोचेमेदिन्द्रंvocemedindraṃ ...... ||
We will address this liberal Lord, this Indra, that he may grant us gifts of ample riches,
Best favourer of the singer's prayer and praises. Preserve us evermore, ye Gods, with blessings.
