HYMN VIII. AGNI

इन्धेindhe राजाrājā समर्योsamaryo नमोभिर्यस्यnamobhiryasya परतीकमाहुतंpratīkamāhutaṃ घर्तेनghṛtena |

नरोnaro हव्येभिरीळतेhavyebhirīḷate सबाधsabādha आग्निरग्रāghniraghra उषसामशोचिuṣasāmaśoci ||
THE King whose face is decked with oil is kindled with homage offered by his faithful servant.
The men, the priests adore him with oblations. Agni hath shone forth when the dawn is breaking.
7.8.1
अयमुayamu षयṣya सुमहानवेदिsumahānavedi होताhotā मन्द्रोmandro मनुषोmanuṣo यज्वोyajvo अग्निःaghniḥ |

विvi भाbhā अकःakaḥ सस्र्जानःsasṛjānaḥ पर्थिव्यांpṛthivyāṃ कर्ष्णपविरोषधीभिर्ववक्षेkṛṣṇapaviroṣadhībhirvavakṣe ||
Yea, he hath been acknowledged as most mighty, the joyous Priest of men, the youthful Agni.
He, spreading o’er the earth, made light around him, and grew among the plants with blackened fellies..
7.8.2
कयाkayā नोno अग्नेaghne विvi वसःvasaḥ सुव्र्क्तिंsuvṛktiṃ कामुkāmu सवधांsvadhāṃ रणवःṛṇavaḥ शस्यमानःśasyamānaḥ |

कदाkadā भवेमbhavema पतयहpatayah सुदत्रsudatra रायोrāyo वन्तारोvantāro दुष्टरस्यduṣṭarasya साधोःsādhoḥ ||
How dost thou decorate our hymn, O Agni? What power dost thou exert when thou art lauded?
When, Bounteous God, may we be lords of riches, winners of precious wealth which none may conquer?
7.8.3
पर-परायमग्निर्भरतस्यpra-prāyamaghnirbharatasya शर्ण्वेśṛṇve विvi यतyat सूर्योsūryo नna रोचतेब्र्हदrocatebṛhad भाःbhāḥ |

अभिabhi यःyaḥ पूरुंpūruṃ पर्तनासुpṛtanāsu तस्थौtasthau दयुतानोdyutāno दैव्योdaivyo अतिथिःatithiḥ शुशोचśuśoca ||
Far famed is this the Bhārata's own Agni he shineth like the Sun with lofty splendour.
He who hath vanquished Pūru in the battle, the heavenly guest hath glowed in full refulgence.
7.8.4
असन्नितasannit तवेtve आहवनानिāhavanāni भूरिbhūri भुवोbhuvo विश्वेभिःviśvebhiḥ सुमनाsumanā अनीकैःanīkaiḥ |

सतुतश्चिदग्नेstutaścidaghne शर्ण्विषेśṛṇviṣe गर्णानःghṛṇānaḥ सवयंsvayaṃ वर्धस्वvardhasva तन्वंtanvaṃ सुजातsujāta ||
Full many oblations are in thee collected: with all thine aspects thou hast waxen gracious.
Thou art already famed as praised and lauded, yet still, O nobly born, increase thy body.
7.8.5
इदंidaṃ वचःvacaḥ शतसाःśatasāḥ संसहस्रमुदग्नयेsaṃsahasramudaghnaye जनिषीष्टjaniṣīṣṭa दविबर्हाःdvibarhāḥ |

शंśaṃ यतyat सतोत्र्भ्यstotṛbhya आपयेāpaye भवातिbhavāti दयुमदमीवचातनंdyumadamīvacātanaṃ रक्षोहाrakṣohā ||
Be this my song, that winneth countless treasure, engendered with redoubled force for Agni,
That, splendid, chasing sickness, slaying demons, it may delight our friend and bless the singers.
7.8.6
नूnū तवामग्नtvāmaghna ईमहेīmahe ...... ||
We, the Vasisthas, now implore thee, Agni, O Son of Strength, the Lord of wealth and riches.
Thou hast brought food to singers and to nobles. Ye Gods, preserve us evermore with blessings.
7.8.7
