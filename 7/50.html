HYMN L. VARIOUS DEITIES.

आā मांmāṃ मित्रावरुणेहmitrāvaruṇeha रक्षतंrakṣataṃ कुलाययदkulāyayad विश्वयनviśvayan माmā नna आā गनghan |

अजकावंajakāvaṃ दुर्द्र्शीकंdurdṛśīkaṃ तिरोtiro दधेdadhe माmā मांmāṃ पद्येनpadyena रपसाrapasā विदतvidat तसरुःtsaruḥ ||
O MITRA-VARUNA, guard and protect me here: let not that come to me which nests within and swells.
I drive afar the scorpion hateful to the sight: let not the winding worm touch me and wound my foot.
7.50.1
यदyad विजामनvijāman परुषिparuṣi वन्दनंvandanaṃ भुवदष्ठीवन्तौbhuvadaṣṭhīvantau परिpari कुल्फौkulphau चca देहतdehat |

अग्निषaghniṣ टच्छोचन्नपṭacchocannapa बाधतामितोbādhatāmito माmā माम्पद्येनmāmpadyena ...... ||
Eruption that appears upon the twofold joints, and that which overspreads the ankles and the knees,
May the refulgent Agni banish far away let not the winding worm touch me and wound my foot.
7.50.2
यच्छल्मलौyacchalmalau भवतिbhavati यनyan नदीषुnadīṣu यदोषधीभ्यःyadoṣadhībhyaḥ परिpari जायतेjāyate विषमviṣam |

विश्वेviśve देवाdevā निरितस्ततniritastat सुवन्तुsuvantu माmā मांmāṃ पद्येनpadyena ...... ||
The poison that is formed upon the Salmali, that which is found in streams, that which the plants produce,
All this may all the Gods banish and drive away: let not the winding worm touch me and wound my foot.
7.50.3
याःyāḥ परवतोpravato निवतnivata उद्वतudvata उदन्वतीरनुदकाश्चudanvatīranudakāśca याःyāḥ |

ताtā अस्मभ्यंasmabhyaṃ पयसाpayasā पिन्वमानाःpinvamānāḥ शिवाśivā देवीरशिपदाdevīraśipadā भवन्तुbhavantu सर्वाsarvā नद्योnadyo अशिमिदाaśimidā भवन्तुbhavantu ||
The steep declivities, the valleys, and the heights, the channels full of water, and the waterless-
May those who swell with water, gracious Goddesses, never afflict us with the Sipada disease, may all the rivers keep us free from Simida.
