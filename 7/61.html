HYMN LXI. MITRA-VARUṆA.

उदud वांvāṃ चक्षुर्वरुणcakṣurvaruṇa सुप्रतीकंsupratīkaṃ देवयोरेतिdevayoreti सूर्यस्ततन्वानsūryastatanvān |

अभिabhi योyo विश्वाviśvā भुवनानिbhuvanāni चष्टेcaṣṭe सsa मन्युंmanyuṃ मर्त्येष्वाmartyeṣvā चिकेतciketa ||
O VARUNA and Mitra, Sūrya spreading the beauteous light of you Twain Gods ariseth.
He who beholdetb all existing creatures observetb well the zeal that is in mortals.
7.61.1
परpra वांvāṃ सsa मित्रावरुणावmitrāvaruṇāv रतावाṛtāvā विप्रोvipro मन्मानिmanmāni दीर्घश्रुदियर्तिdīrghaśrudiyarti |

यस्यyasya बरह्माणिbrahmāṇi सुक्रतूsukratū अवाथavātha आā यतyat करत्वाkratvā नna शरदःśaradaḥ पर्णैथेpṛṇaithe ||
The holy sage, renowned afar, directeth his hymns to you, O Varuṇa and Mitra,—
He whose devotions, sapient Gods, ye favour so that ye fill, as ’twere, with power his autumns.
7.61.2
परोरोर्मित्रावरुणाprorormitrāvaruṇā पर्थिव्याःpṛthivyāḥ परpra दिवdiva रष्वादṛṣvād बर्हतःbṛhataḥ सुदानूsudānū |

सपशोspaśo दधाथेdadhāthe ओषधीषुoṣadhīṣu विक्ष्वvikṣv रधगṛdhagh यतोyato अनिमिषंरक्षमाणाanimiṣaṃrakṣamāṇā ||
From the wide earth, O Varuṇa and Mitra from the great lofty heaven, ye, Bounteous Givers, -
Have in the fields and houses set your warder-, who visit every spot and watch unceasing.
7.61.3
शंसाśaṃsā मित्रस्यmitrasya वरुणस्यvaruṇasya धामdhāma शुष्मोśuṣmo रोदसीrodasī बद्बधेbadbadhe महित्वाmahitvā |

अयनayan मासाmāsā अयज्वनामवीराःayajvanāmavīrāḥ परpra यज्ञमन्माyajñamanmā वर्जनंvṛjanaṃ तिरातेtirāte ||
I praise the strength of Varuṇa and Mitra that strength, by mightiness, keeps both worlds asunder.
Heroless pass the months of the ungodly he who loves sacrifice makes his home enduring.
7.61.4
अमूराamūrā विश्वाviśvā वर्षणाविमाvṛṣaṇāvimā वांvāṃ नna यासुyāsu चित्रंcitraṃ दद्र्शेनdadṛśena यक्षमyakṣam |

दरुहःdruhaḥ सचन्तेsacante अन्र्ताanṛtā जनानांjanānāṃ नna वांvāṃ निण्यान्यचितेniṇyānyacite अभूवनabhūvan ||
Steers, all infallible are these your people in whom no wondrous thing is seen, no worship.
Guile follows close the men who are untruthful: no secrets may be hidden from your knowledge.
7.61.5
समुsamu वांvāṃ यज्ञंyajñaṃ महयंmahayaṃ नमोभिर्हुवेnamobhirhuve वांvāṃ मित्रावरुणाmitrāvaruṇā सबाधःsabādhaḥ |

परpra वांvāṃ मन्मान्यmanmāny रचसेṛcase नवानिnavāni कर्तानिkṛtāni बरह्मbrahma जुजुषन्निमानिjujuṣannimāni ||
I will exalt your sacrifice with homage: as priest, I, Mitra-Varuṇa, invoke you.
May these new hymns and prayers that I have fashioned delight you to the profit of the singer.
7.61.6
इयंiyaṃ देवdeva पुरोहितिर...purohitir... ||
This priestly task, Gods! Varuṇa and Mitra! hath been performed for you at sacrifices.
Convey us safely over every peril. Preserve us evermore, ye Gods, with blessings.
