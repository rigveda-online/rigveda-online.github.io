HYMN XLVI. RUDRA.

इमाimā रुद्रायrudrāya सथिरधन्वनेsthiradhanvane गिरःghiraḥ कषिप्रेषवेkṣipreṣave देवायdevāya सवधाव्नेsvadhāvne |

अषाळ्हायaṣāḷhāya सहमानायsahamānāya वेधसेvedhase तिग्मायुधायtighmāyudhāya भरताbharatā शर्णोतुśṛṇotu नःnaḥ ||
To Rudra bring these songs, whose bow is firm and strong, the self-dependent God with swiftly-flying shafts,
The Wise, the Conqueror whom none may overcome, armed with sharp-pointed weapons: may he hear our call.
7.46.1
सsa हिhi कषयेणkṣayeṇa कषम्यस्यkṣamyasya जन्मनःjanmanaḥ साम्राज्येनsāmrājyena दिव्यस्यdivyasya चेततिcetati |

अवन्नवन्तीरुपavannavantīrupa नोno दुरश्चरानमीवोduraścarānamīvo रुद्रrudra जासुjāsu नोno भवbhava ||
He through his lordship thinks on beings of the earth, on heavenly beings through his high imperial sway.
Come willingly to our doors that gladly welcome thee, and heal all sickness, Rudra., in our families.
7.46.2
याyā तेte दिद्युदवस्र्ष्टाdidyudavasṛṣṭā दिवसdivas परिpari कष्मयाkṣmayā चरतिcarati परिpari साव्र्णक्तुsāvṛṇaktu नःnaḥ |

सहस्रंsahasraṃ तेte सवपिवातsvapivāta भेषजाbheṣajā माmā नस्तोकेषुतनयेषुnastokeṣutanayeṣu रीरिषहrīriṣah ||
May thy bright arrow which, shot down by thee from heaven, flieth upon the earth, pass us uninjured by.
Thou, very gracious God, hast thousand medicines: inflict no evil on our sons or progeny.
7.46.3
माmā नोno वधीvadhī रुद्रrudra माmā पराparā दाdā माmā तेte भूमbhūma परसितौprasitau हीळितस्यhīḷitasya |

आā नोno भजbhaja बर्हिषिbarhiṣi जीवशंसेjīvaśaṃse यूयंyūyaṃ पातpāta ...... ||
Slay us not, nor abandon us, O Rudra let not thy noose, when thou art angry, seize us.
Give us trimmed grass and fame among the living. Preserve us evermore, ye Gods, with blessings.
