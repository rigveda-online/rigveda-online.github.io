HYMN CII PARJANYA.

पर्जन्यायparjanyāya परpra गायतghāyata दिवसdivas पुत्रायputrāya मीळ्हुषेmīḷhuṣe |

सsa नोno यवसमिछतुyavasamichatu ||
SING forth and laud Parjanya, son of Heaven, who sends the gift of rain
May he provide our pasturage.
7.102.1
योyo गर्भमोषधीनांgharbhamoṣadhīnāṃ गवांghavāṃ कर्णोत्यर्वतामkṛṇotyarvatām |

पर्जन्यःपुरुषीणामparjanyaḥpuruṣīṇām ||
Parjanya is the God who forms in kine, in mares, in plants of earth,
And womankind, the germ of life.
7.102.2
तस्माtasmā इदास्येidāsye हविर्जुहोताhavirjuhotā मधुमत्तममmadhumattamam |

इळांiḷāṃ नःnaḥ संयतंsaṃyataṃ करतkarat ||
Offer and pour into his mouth oblation rich in savoury juice:
May he for ever give us food.
7.102.3
