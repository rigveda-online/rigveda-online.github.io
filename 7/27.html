HYMN XXVII. INDRA.

इन्द्रंindraṃ नरोnaro नेमधिताnemadhitā हवन्तेhavante यतyat पार्याpāryā युनजतेyunajate धियस्ताःdhiyastāḥ |

शूरोśūro नर्षाताnṛṣātā शवसश्चकानśavasaścakāna आā गोमतिghomati वरजेvraje भजात्वंbhajātvaṃ नःnaḥ ||
MEN call on Indra in the armed encounter that he may make the hymns they sing decisive.
Hero, rejoicing in thy might, in combat give us a portion of the stall of cattle,
7.27.1
यya इन्द्रindra शुष्मोśuṣmo मघवनmaghavan तेte अस्तिasti शिक्षाśikṣā सखिभ्यःsakhibhyaḥ पुरुहूतन्र्भ्यःpuruhūtanṛbhyaḥ |

तवंtvaṃ हिhi दर्ळ्हाdṛḷhā मघवनmaghavan विचेताvicetā अपाapā वर्धिvṛdhi परिव्र्तंparivṛtaṃ नna राधःrādhaḥ ||
Grant, Indra Maghavan, invoked of many, to these my friends the strength which thou possessest.
Thou, Maghavan, hast rent strong places open: unclose for us, Wise God, thy hidden bounty.
7.27.2
इन्द्रोindro राजाrājā जगतश्चर्षणीनामधिjaghataścarṣaṇīnāmadhi कषमिkṣami विषुरूपंviṣurūpaṃ यदस्तिyadasti |

ततोtato ददातिdadāti दाशुषेdāśuṣe वसूनिvasūni चोददcodad राधrādha उपस्तुतश्चिदर्वाकupastutaścidarvāk ||
King of the living world, of men, is Indra, of all in varied form that earth containeth.
Thence to the worshipper he giveth riches: may he enrich us also when we laud him.
7.27.3
नूnū चिनcin नna इन्द्रोindro मघवाmaghavā सहूतीsahūtī दानोdāno वाजंvājaṃ निni यमतेyamate नna ऊतीūtī |

अनूनाanūnā यस्यyasya दक्षिणाdakṣiṇā पीपायpīpāya वामंvāmaṃ नर्भ्योnṛbhyo अभिवीताabhivītā सखिभ्यःsakhibhyaḥ ||
Maghavan Indra, when we all invoke him, bountiful ever sendeth strength to aid us:
Whose perfect guerdon, never failing, bringeth wealth to the men, to friends the thing they covet.
7.27.4
नूnū इन्द्रindra रायेrāye वरिवसvarivas कर्धीkṛdhī नna आā तेte मनोmano वव्र्त्यामvavṛtyāma मघायmaghāya |

गोमदश्वावदghomadaśvāvad रथवदrathavad वयन्तोvyanto यूयंyūyaṃ पातpāta ...... ||
Quick, Indra, give us room and way to riches, and let us bring thy mind to grant us treasures,
That we may win us cars and Steeds and cattle. Preserve us evermore, ye Gods, with blessings.
7.27.5
