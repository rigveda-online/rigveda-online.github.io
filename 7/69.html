HYMN LXIX. AŚVINS.

आā वांvāṃ रथोratho रोदसीrodasī बद्बधानोbadbadhāno हिरण्ययोhiraṇyayo वर्षभिर्यात्वश्वैःvṛṣabhiryātvaśvaiḥ |

घर्तवर्तनिःghṛtavartaniḥ पविभीpavibhī रुचानrucāna इषांiṣāṃ वोळ्हाvoḷhā नर्पतिर्वाजिनीवानnṛpatirvājinīvān ||
MAY your gold chariot, drawn by vigorous horses, come to us, blocking up the earth
and heaven,
Bright with its fellies while its way drops fatness, food-laden, rich in coursers, man's protector.
7.69.1
सsa पप्रथानोpaprathāno अभिabhi पञ्चpañca भूमाbhūmā तरिवन्धुरोtrivandhuro मनसाmanasā यातुyātu युक्तःyuktaḥ |

विशोviśo येनyena गछथोghachatho देवयन्तीःdevayantīḥ कुत्राkutrā चिदcid याममश्विनाyāmamaśvinā दधानाdadhānā ||
Let it approach, yoked by thewill, three-seated, extending far and wide o’er fivefold beings,
Whereon ye visit God-adoring races, bending your course whither ye will, O Aśvins.
7.69.2
सवश्वाsvaśvā यशसाyaśasā यातमर्वागyātamarvāgh दस्राdasrā निधिंnidhiṃ मधुमन्तंmadhumantaṃ पिबाथःpibāthaḥ |

विvi वांvāṃ रथोratho वध्वाvadhvā यादमानो.अन्तानyādamāno.antān दिवोdivo बाधतेbādhate वर्तनिभ्यामvartanibhyām ||
Renowned, with noble horses, come ye hither: drink, Wondrous Pair, the cup that holds sweet juices.
Your car whereon your Spouse is wont to travel marks with its track the farthest ends of heaven.
7.69.3
युवोःyuvoḥ शरियंśriyaṃ परिpari योषाव्र्णीतyoṣāvṛṇīta सूरोsūro दुहिताduhitā परितक्म्यायामparitakmyāyām |

यदyad देवयन्तमवथःdevayantamavathaḥ शचीभिःśacībhiḥ परिpari घरंसमोमनाghraṃsamomanā वांvāṃ वयोvayo गातghāt ||
When night was turning to the grey of morning the Maiden, Sūrya's Daughter, chose your splendour.
When with your power and might ye aid the pious he comes through heat to life by your assistance.
7.69.4
योyo हha सयsya वांvāṃ रथिराrathirā वस्तvasta उस्राusrā रथोratho युजानःyujānaḥ परियातिवर्तिःpariyātivartiḥ |

तेनtena नःnaḥ शंśaṃ योरुषसोyoruṣaso वयुष्टौvyuṣṭau नयश्विनाnyaśvinā वहतंvahataṃ यज्ञेyajñe अस्मिनasmin ||
O Chariot-borne, this car of yours invested with rays of light comes harnessed to our dwelling.
Herewith, O Aśvins, while the dawn is breaking, to this our sacrifice bring peace and blessing.
7.69.5
नराnarā गौरेवghaureva विद्युतंvidyutaṃ तर्षाणास्माकमद्यtṛṣāṇāsmākamadya सवनोपsavanopa यातमyātam |

पुरुत्राpurutrā हिhi वांvāṃ मतिभिर्हवन्तेmatibhirhavante माmā वामन्येvāmanye निni यमनyaman देवयन्तःdevayantaḥ ||
Like the wild cattle thirsty for the lightning, Heroes, come nigh this day to our libations.
Men call on you with hymns in many places, but let not other worshippers detain you.
7.69.6
युवंyuvaṃ भुज्युमवविद्धंbhujyumavaviddhaṃ समुद्रsamudra उदूहथुरर्णसोudūhathurarṇaso अस्रिधानैःasridhānaiḥ |

पतत्रिभिरश्रमैरव्यथिभिर्दंसनाभिरश्विनाpatatribhiraśramairavyathibhirdaṃsanābhiraśvinā पारयन्ताpārayantā ||
Bhujyu, abandoned in the midst of ocean, ye raised from out the water with your horses,
Uninjured, winged, flagging not, undaunted, with deeds of wonder saving him, O Aśvins.
7.69.7
नूnū मेme हवमाhavamā शर्णुतंśṛṇutaṃ युवानाyuvānā ...... ||
Now hear, O Youthful Twain, mine invocation: come, Aśvins, to the home where food aboundeth.
Vouchsafe us wealth, do honour to our nobles. Preserve us evermore, ye Gods, with blessings.
7.69.8
