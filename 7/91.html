HYMN XCI. VĀYU.

कुविदङगkuvidaṅgha नमसाnamasā येye वर्धासःvṛdhāsaḥ पुराpurā देवाdevā अनवद्यासanavadyāsa आसनāsan |

तेte वायवेvāyave मनवेmanave बाधितायावासयन्नुषसंbādhitāyāvāsayannuṣasaṃ सूर्येणsūryeṇa ||
WERE not in sooth, the Gods aforetime blameless, whose pleasure was increased by adoration?
For Vāyu and for man in his affliction they caused the Morning to arise with Sūrya.
7.91.1
उशन्ताuśantā दूताdūtā नna दभायdabhāya गोपाghopā मासश्चmāsaśca पाथःpāthaḥ शरदश्चśaradaśca पूर्वीःpūrvīḥ |

इन्द्रवायूindravāyū सुष्टुतिर्वामियानाsuṣṭutirvāmiyānā मार्डीकमीट्टेmārḍīkamīṭṭe सुवितंsuvitaṃ चca नव्यमnavyam ||
Guardians infallible, eager as envoys' preserve us safe through many months and autumns.
Addressed to you, our fair praise, Indra-Vāyu, implores your favour and renewed well-being.
7.91.2
पीवोन्नानpīvoannān रयिव्र्धःrayivṛdhaḥ सुमेधाःsumedhāḥ शवेतःśvetaḥ सिषक्तिsiṣakti नियुतामभिश्रीःniyutāmabhiśrīḥ |

तेte वायवेvāyave समनसोsamanaso विvi तस्थुर्विश्वेनtasthurviśven नरःnaraḥ सवपत्यानिsvapatyāni चक्रुःcakruḥ ||
Wise, bright, arranger of his teams, he. seeketh men with rich food whose treasures are abundant.
They have arranged them of one mind with Vāyu: the men have wrought all noble operations.
7.91.3
यावतyāvat तरस्तन्वोtarastanvo यावदोजोyāvadojo यावनyāvan नरश्चक्षसाnaraścakṣasā दीध्यानाःdīdhyānāḥ |

शुचिंśuciṃ सोमंsomaṃ शुचिपाśucipā पातमस्मेpātamasme इन्द्रवायूindravāyū सदतम्बर्हिरेदमsadatambarhiredam ||
So far as native power and strength permit you, so far as men behold whose eyes have vision,
O ye pure-drinkers, drink with us pure Soma: sit on this sacred grass, Indra and Vāyu.
7.91.4
नियुवानाniyuvānā नियुतniyuta सपार्हवीराspārhavīrā इन्द्रविindravi -मर्वाक-amarvāk | आू}āū} इदंidaṃ हिhi वांvāṃ परभ्र्तंprabhṛtaṃ मध्वोmadhvo अग्रमधaghramadha परीणानाprīṇānā विमुमुक्तमस्मेvimumuktamasme ||
Driving down teams that bear the lovely Heroes, hitherward, Indra-Vāyu, come together.
To you this prime of savoury juice is offered: here loose your horses and be friendly-minded.
7.91.5
याyā वांvāṃ शतंśataṃ नियुतोniyuto याःyāḥ सहस्रमिन्द्रवायूsahasramindravāyū विश्ववाराःviśvavārāḥ सचन्तेsacante |

आभिर्यातंābhiryātaṃ सुविदत्राभिरर्वाकsuvidatrābhirarvāk पातंpātaṃ नराप्रतिभ्र्तस्यnarāpratibhṛtasya मध्वःmadhvaḥ ||
Your hundred and your thousand teams, O Indra and Vāyu, all-munificent, which attend you,
With these most gracious-minded come ye hither, and drink, O Heroes of the meath we offer.
7.91.6
अर्वन्तोarvanto नna शरवसोśravaso ...... ||
Like coursers seeking fame will we Vasisthas, O Indra-Vāyu, with our fair laudations,
Exerting all our powe-,, call you to aid us. Preserve us evermore, ye Gods, with blessings.
