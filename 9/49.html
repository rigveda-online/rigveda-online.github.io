HYMN XLIX. SOMA PAVAMANA.

पवस्वpavasva वर्ष्टिमाvṛṣṭimā सुsu नो.अपामूर्मिंno.apāmūrmiṃ दिवसdivas परिpari |

अयक्ष्माayakṣmā बर्हतीरिषःbṛhatīriṣaḥ ||
Poust down the rain upon us, pour a wave of waters from the sky,
And plenteous store of wholesome fbod.
9.49.1
तयाtayā पवस्वpavasva धारयाdhārayā ययाyayā गावghāva इहागमनihāghaman |

जन्यासjanyāsa उपupa नोno गर्हमghṛham ||
Flow onward with that stream of thine, whereby the cows have come to us,
The kine of strangers to our home.
9.49.2
घर्तंghṛtaṃ पवस्वpavasva धारयाdhārayā यज्ञेषुyajñeṣu देववीतमःdevavītamaḥ |

अस्मभ्यंasmabhyaṃ वर्ष्टिमाvṛṣṭimā पवpava ||
Chief Friend of Gods in sacred rites, pour on us fatness with thy stream,
Ppur down on us a flood of rain.
9.49.3
सsa नna ऊर्जेūrje वयव्ययंvyavyayaṃ पवित्रंpavitraṃ धावdhāva धारयाdhārayā |

देवासःdevāsaḥ शर्णवनśṛṇavan हिhi कमkam ||
To give us vigour, with thy stream run through the fleecy straining-cloth
For verily the Gods will bear.
9.49.4
पवमानोpavamāno असिष्यददasiṣyadad रक्षांस्यपजङघनतrakṣāṃsyapajaṅghanat |

परत्नवदpratnavad रोचयनrocayan रुचःrucaḥ ||
Onward hath Pavamana flowed and beaten off the Rākṣasas,
Flashing out splendour as of old.
