HYMN XCIII. SOMA PAVAMANA.

साकमुक्षोsākamukṣo मर्जयन्तmarjayanta सवसारोsvasāro दशdaśa धीरस्यdhīrasya धीतयोdhītayo धनुत्रीःdhanutrīḥ |

हरिःhariḥ पर्यद्रवज्जाःparyadravajjāḥ सूर्यस्यsūryasya दरोणंdroṇaṃ ननक्षेnanakṣe अत्योatyo नna वाजीvājī ||
TEN sisters, pouring out the rain together, swift-moving thinkers of the sage, adorn him.
Hither hath run the gold-hued Child of Sūrya and reached the vat like a fleet vigorous courser.
9.93.1
संsaṃ मात्र्भिर्नmātṛbhirna शिशुर्वावशानोśiśurvāvaśāno वर्षाvṛṣā दधन्वेdadhanve पुरुवारोद्भिःpuruvāroadbhiḥ |

मर्योmaryo नna योषामभिyoṣāmabhi निष्क्र्तंniṣkṛtaṃ यनyan संsaṃ गछतेghachate कलशkalaśa उस्रियाभिःusriyābhiḥ ||
Even as a youngling crying to his mothers, the bounteous Steer hath flowed along to waters.
As youth to damsel, so with milk he hastens on to the. chose meeting-place, the beaker.
9.93.2
उतuta परpra पिप्यpipya ऊधरघ्न्यायाūdharaghnyāyā इन्दुर्धाराभिःindurdhārābhiḥ सचतेsacate सुमेधाःsumedhāḥ |

मूर्धानंmūrdhānaṃ गावःghāvaḥ पयसाpayasā चमूष्वभिcamūṣvabhi शरीणन्तिśrīṇanti वसुभिर्नvasubhirna निक्तैःniktaiḥ ||
Yea, swollen is the udder of the milch-cow: thither in streams goes very sapient Indu.
The kine make ready, as with new-washed treasures, the Head and Chief with milk within the vessels.
9.93.3
सsa नोno देवेभिःdevebhiḥ पवमानpavamāna रदेन्दोradendo रयिमश्विनंrayimaśvinaṃ वावशानःvāvaśānaḥ |

रथिरायतामुशतीrathirāyatāmuśatī पुरन्धिरस्मद्र्यगाpurandhirasmadryaghā दावनेdāvane वसूनामvasūnām ||
With all the Gods, O Indu Pavamana, while thou art roaring send us wealth in horses.
Hither upon her car come willing Plenty, inclined to us, to give us of her treasures.
9.93.4
नूnū नोno रयिमुपrayimupa मास्वmāsva नर्वन्तंnṛvantaṃ पुनानोpunāno वाताप्यंvātāpyaṃ विश्वश्चन्द्रमviśvaścandram |

परpra वन्दितुरिन्दोvanditurindo तार्यायुःtāryāyuḥ परातर्मक्षूprātarmakṣū धियावसुर्जगम्यातdhiyāvasurjaghamyāt ||
Now unto us mete riches, while they cleansethee, all-glorious, swelling wealth, with store of heroes.
Long be his life who worships, thee, O Indu. May he, enriched with prayer, come soon and early.
9.93.5
